# ettuge — Kannada Linguistics

**Dr. D.N. Shankara Bhat** is one of India's foremost typological linguists. He was a professor at CIIL Mysore, a fellow of the Linguistic Society of America, and the author of over 20 books on Kannada grammar, phonology, and general linguistics.

This site hosts study materials for his Kannada-language works — English summaries, **Eke** romanisation, and source texts — for researchers, students, and Kannada language enthusiasts.

## Eke Romanisation

All Kannada text is also available in **Eke** — a phonemic romanisation for Kannada designed by Vishwas. Key conventions:

| Feature | Eke | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|
| Long vowels | Uppercase | `A I U E O` → ಆ ಈ ಊ ಏ ಓ |
| Retroflexes | Uppercase | `T D N L S` → ಟ ಡ ಣ ಳ ಶ/ಷ |
| Diphthongs | `ay`, `av` | `aydu` → ಐದು, `avSaDa` → ಔಷಧ |
| Vocalic ṛ | `x` / `X` | `samskxta` → ಸಂಸ್ಕೃತ |
| Anusvara | assimilated | `kamba` ಕಂಬ, `kanTha` ಕಂಠ, `samskxta` |

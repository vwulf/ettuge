## What Can Be Inferred

The title ಕನ್ನಡ ಬರಹದ ಪದಸಮಸ್ಯೆ means "The word-problem of Kannada writing" — ಪದಸಮಸ್ಯೆ (pada + samasye) = the problem of words/vocabulary in written Kannada. This is the same subject as Chapter 5 of Book 30 (ಕನ್ನಡ ಬರಹವನ್ನು ಸರಿಪಡಿಸೋಣ), which is titled identically, suggesting Book 11 may be an earlier, standalone treatment of the same topic that was later incorporated into Book 30.

The ಪದಸಮಸ್ಯೆ (word problem) in DNS Bhat's framework refers to the problem of Kannada writers borrowing Sanskrit vocabulary rather than using or coining native Dravidian words. The "word problem" thus has two aspects:
1. Existing Sanskrit loanwords dominating written Kannada prose
2. New technical/modern vocabulary being created from Sanskrit roots instead of native Kannada roots

From the single usable fragment (Part 8): "ಈ ಪ್ರಕರಣ ಅಥವಾ ನಾವು ಕರೆಯುವ ಅಧ್ಯಾಯ… ಕೆಲವು ಪ್ರಸ್ತುತ ಲೇಖಕರು ಎಂತಹ ಕೋನದಿಂದ" — "this chapter / what angle some present-day writers take" — suggests Bhat is evaluating how contemporary Kannada writers handle the word-formation problem.

The 25-part structure suggests this is one of the longer books in the series (comparable in length to Book 03 with its 33+ parts).

## viShaya 1 — kannaDaddE vyAkaraNa: samskxta sollarime alla

*bhAga 1*

[📼 bhAga 1 →](../kn/full#part-1)

naDeyuva kannaDa sollarimegaLu nija kannaDa sollarimegaLalla — avugaLu samskxta pANinI vyAkaraNa niyamagaLannu kannaDa mElE hEruttave. kannaDa draviDa bhAShe; samskxta indo-yUrOpiyan bhAShe — eraDara iTTaLa bahaLa bErE. I tappAda A daLike halavAru varSagaLindA naDeyuttidE.

DNS baT avaru 2010–2019ra naDuvE 'kannaDa barahada sollarime' enba 7 saMpuTagaLalli nija kannaDa vyAkaraNa baredaru. A kAlasada sAravE I 2021ra ondu saMpuTa 'idu kannaDaddE vyAkaraNa' (253 puttaLagaLu). muKya viShaya ondu: vyAkaraNa embudu hEraDininda baru kalisuvudu alla — prateya kannaDiga Agale tamma bhASheya niyamagaLannu ariyuttAne; vyAkaraNa A niyamagaLannu paTTi heLayuttade.

kannaDa vyAkaraNa odidarE eraDu badalAvaNegaLannu mADabEku:
(1) samskxta eravalu padagaLannu kaDime mADi drAviDa mUlada padagaLannu baLasabEku
(2) uLida eravalu padagaLannu kannaDigaru uccAraNe mADuvante bareyabEku

> *(bhAga 1ralli oLapiDi odi keLabAhudu — 19 adhyAyagaLa hEru)*

## viShaya 2 — ellara kannaDa: 31 barige vyavasthe

*bhAga 1–2*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 1–2 →](../kn/full#part-1)

barahada kannaDakke ondu prAmANika rUpa bEku: I kAraNakke baT avaru 'ellara kannaDa' vyavaste mADidAre. I vyavasteyalli 31 barigegaLu — 10 svaravuLigaLu (tereyuLi / igi) mattu 21 vyanjana gaLu (muccuyuLi). I saMkhye samskxta mahAprANa barigegaLannu mattu reTroflex sa (S) avugaLannu bAdi mADuttade.

barahada kannaDa ADuhADida kannaDiganige heccu prAdesika bhEda irade biDuttade — I kAraNakke 'ellara kannaDa' barahakke heccu sAkShiyAguttade. ADu kannaDa prAdEsika oLanuDigaLAgi haLeyaguttuTTade; baraha mAtra sAmagra kannaDa samudAyakke SeEva sALuttade.

## viShaya 3 — padagaLa iTTaLa: hesaru, esaka, paricaya padagaLu

*bhAga 1–2*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 1–2 →](../kn/full#part-1)

kannaDa padagaLannu mUru muKya vagegaLAgi haliyabahude:

**hesaru padagaLu (§1.2.3):** vyakti, vastu, parikalpanegaLannu hesarisuvudu — 'rAju', 'manE', 'nIru' ivugaLu. eraDu bhEda: koTTa hesaru (prAvAcaka — jAnaki, maisUru) mattu iruvA hesaru (sAmAnya — mara, huDuga).

**esaka padagaLu (§1.2.4):** AgOluha, mADoluha, iroluha emba esaTagaLannu tOruttave — 'bA', 'mADu', 'iru' ivugaLu.

**paricaya padagaLu (§1.2.5):** hesaru padakke horaTu iruvA padagaLu — 'citta', 'hoLe', 'mEle' ivugaLu.

horaTu: ADu padagaLu (nAnu, nInu), tOru padagaLu (idu/adu, ivaru/avaru), eNike padagaLu, aNaka padagaLu (dagadagane uriyu, DabbanebiTTu).

iTTaLaviruvA padagaLu: cikkappa = cikka + appa. iTTaLavillada padagaLu: tale, manE — biDisikOLLanE. jODu padagaLu: cikkanna (tande tanammana kiru tammanige hesaru — 'cikka tamme' emba kEvaLa artha alla).

## viShaya 4 — padarUpagaLu mattu padakante iTTaLa

*bhAga 2–3*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 2–3 →](../kn/full#part-2)

**padarUpagaLu (§1.2.7):** hesaru padagaLu vibhaktigonDu rUpabadalAvaNe mADikOLLuttave: mara (prathamE), maravanu (karmE), maradinda (apAdAna), marakke (sampradAna), maradalli (adhikaraNa), marada (ShaSThI). esaka padagaLu heccu vistaraNeyAgi rUpabadalAvaNe mADikOLLuttave: haTTida (bhUta, purushakartha), haTTitu (bhUta, nirjIva), haTTuttade (bartamAna/bhaviShya), haTTali (prArthanE), haTTisidaru (prEraNA bhUta).

**padakante iTTaLa (§1.2.8):** 'cikka huDuga hattu roTTi tinda' emba vAkyada iTTaLa: [cikka huDuga] + [hattu roTTi] + tinda. pada maTTadalli vAkyave artha koDuvudilla — padakante maTTadalli. 'huli bAyige bidda DhanaDa karu kUgADuttide' emba vAkyadalli eraDu artha sambhava: I tantrAMSa iTTaLada sAkSi.

## viShaya 5 — vAkya iTTaLa: pAngu mattu vAkya vagegaLu

*bhAga 3*

[📼 bhAga 3 →](../kn/full#part-3)

**vAkya vagegaLu (§1.3):** suLu vAkyagaLu (kEvaLa ondu esaka pada), jODu vAkyagaLu (eraDu suLu vAkyagaLa jODaNe), sikkalu vAkyagaLu (ondu vAkyadoLage innondannu seresikoLLuvudu).

**pAngugaLu (§1.3.2):** prateya esaka pada tannonDige iruvA kAraka pAngugaLu (participant role arguments) bEDuttade. sUru emba esaka pada: AgOluha (zero-marked) + aDDi pAngu (apAdAna, -inda). mAru emba esaka pada: mADuha (zero-marked) + guri (sampradAna, -ge) + IDu (karmE, -annu). I rIti prateya esaka padakke nirdishTa pAngu iTTaLa iruttade.

pAngu vagegaLu: IDu (karmE, -annu), suru (mUla/apAdAna, -inda), guri (laksya/sampradAna, -ge), jAga (sthAna, -alli), mADuha (kartxvAcaka, zero), AgOluha (kriyAvyApta, zero). I niyamita iTTaLa kannaDa vyAkaraNada muKya daLike.

## viShaya 6 — eraDu suDAraNegaLu: eravalu pada mattu uccAraNe baraha

*bhAga 1 mattu bhAga 3*

[📼 bhAga 1 →](../kn/full#part-1)

kannaDa barahavanu kalisuvaLLi mattu baLasivaLLi I eraDu suDAraNegaLu muKya:

**suDAraNe ondu — eravalu pada kaDime:** samskxta eravalu padagaLannu kaDime mADi drAviDa mUlada padagaLannu baLasabEku. I kAraNakke baT avaru pustakami tumbA svantada native padagaLannu balasidarE (hesaru pada, esaka pada, iTTaLa, pAngu, ottugaLu — ivugaLella drAviDa mUlada). makkaLige I padagaLu mElike hOdare baraha kalivaLLi sUlabavAguttade.

**suDAraNe eraDu — uccAraNe rUpadalli baraha:** samskxta eravalu padagaLannu kannaDigaru hEge uccAraNe mADuttArO hAgeye bareyabEku. 'karma' enba samskxta padavanu 'karma' endu bareyuva bajAyu 'karmA' emba uccAraNe sAriya bareyabEku. I suDAraNe mahAprANa barigegaLannu hEga baLasabEku emba kaTraLigininda muktisuttade.

I eraDu suDAraNegaLannu mADigare kannaDa barahavanu eLLarige kalisuvaLLi sUlabavAguttade — eLLara kannaDigarige baraha talaupuvante mADuvaLE sAmAjika nyAdha.

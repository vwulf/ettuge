## bAga ondu — bAsheya svarUpa

### 1.1 yAvu bAse mElu yA kILu alla {#11}

bAsegaLige nAvu "saraLa", "kaSTa", "sogasAda", "garaTu", "ADi-bArA", "beLeda" aMtella viSeSaNagaLannu koLLuttEve — Adare nuDiyarime (linguistics) saM-sOdane ee guNagaLu bAsegaLa svantakke sErida viSayagaLalla eMdu tORisuttade. ivella keLabEvaru beLedukOMDu baMda vaiyaktika/mAnasika nOTa-buDi-kaLe mAtragaLu. bAse hEge huttittu eMbude kaLiyuvavarige Arambada bAseyE saraLa; iMgindu nODida aTTa kannaDigaLu cInIyannu kaLiyuvudu kannaDiganige kannaD kaLiyuvudu eTTu kaSTavO aTTu kaSTa. saMskRitakke duSTa vyAkaraNa idE, Adare A kaSTatu "vyAkaraNa vaRNane"dalli iddE hordadu, saMskRitannu tAy-nuDigE kandu kaMDu beLeda jana saMskRitannu kaliyadE idu sADisvada. bAseyarimeyavannu ondu bhASanu aTTininda aTTannu prAmANita vargIsalu mADida prayatnagaLella viphalavAdE bAdave. oMdu aMsa (pada-racane) saraLAvAga, iMnoMdu aMsa (vAkya-racane) kaSTAvAguttade; muttavAgi bAsegaLu saMtulanavAgi biDuttave. bAse-ShreNIkrama (linguistic hierarchy) eMbude illa.

### 1.2 bAsegaLa huTTu mattu sAvugaLu {#12}

### 1.3 bAsegaLalli hata mattu hosa — bAse-badalu {#13}

ellA badukunuva bAsegaLu nittya badalaAguttave — ucchAraNe, pada-kOsa, vyAkaraNa talE-talE badalaAguttave. baT avaru kannaD upabAsegaLinda idu tORisuttAre: karaLa kannaDada Havyaka upabAse keMda kannaDigaLu kaLedukOMDa koMDu Hata-kannaD lakSaNagaLannu (-andiru aMta bahuvacana oTTannu uLasikoMDu) uLisikOMDirE, A prade-shadalli hosa lakSaNagaLannu tande-tande beLesikoMDiddE. badalu "hALu" alla; adu avoidable, anivArya, niyama-baddha natanE. "Suddha" bAse eMbude mithya; bAse sthira-niranta iruvudu eMda kAlavu irlilla.

### 1.4 bASA-svAtantrya {#14}

bAsegaLu formal-tarka-parimitiyanalli kaTTubiTTa alla — avugaLalli svAtantrya mattu saMpradAya oMdE sArigirivuvu. jagattinalli yAvu nuDiyariru bEkAdaru mundu kELadE iru vAkyagaLannu sRiSTisalAguvudu; makkaLu karpisidarida niyamagaLannu tiLidukOMDu bAsegaLa vyAkaraNannu hode-hode tiLidukOMDe beLeyuttAre. bAsheya ee sRijana-svAtantrya (baT "bASA-svAtantrya" eMbadu) nirdista bAsE-vAkya-niyamagaLiMda bErE viSaya. bAsegaLu linga-racaneyanalli, kAla-kaMTegaLalli, eMcuva paddhatiyanalli bEre bEre AgiruvudarE "yAvaduE sOdilAda yA tarkA-bhAvadadu" eMdarvudalla.

### 1.5 bAse tarkabaddavE? {#15}

"bAse tarkabadda alla" eMdu janaru hala-gaNTe dUridu-dUridu hELuttAre — iMgliSudi "I went" (not "I goed") yAke? koMDu bAsegaLalli double negation yaMannu gooNisuttadE yAke? baT tORisuttAre: bAse tavaru aMtaranga-tarkavannu anusarIte, adu formal-tarkagiMta bErAgirubahudu. linga-racane (kannaD jIvi/nijIvi bEdadalli, not puM/strI bEda), vacana-vividhate (koMDu bAsegaLalli ondu, eraDu, mUru, nAlku eMtu vibhAgagaLu), mattu kAla-vibhAga (ellA bAsegaLu onEE tarka-kAlAnnu guri-paduvudilla) kurita udAharaNagaLannu koDuttAre. ee vividhatagaLu tarka-kampavalla — bhasitannu kaDeyuva bEre bEre mArgagaLu.

### 1.6 kAla (vartamAna kAla) {#16}

"vartamAna kAla" eMdu nAvu eMbuvadannu nibaMdha oru kroDI-viSayavAgi upayOgisi bAse-dATE kAlika-vividhate tORisuttade. kannaDigaLalli nAvu "vartamAna kAla" eMbadu iMgliSinadu giMta joTilavu: iru-kriyeyoMdige Agalade kArya (present moment), abhyAsa (habitual present), sAmAnya satya eMdu bEre bEre. Manipuriyalli kAla anivArya-baddavAgi tiLisalAguvudu; cIniyalli kriyA-padagaLu kAlakke baddalAguvudilla. "vartamAna kAla" eMdu vyAkaraNakAraru hELuvudu bEre bEre bAsegaLalli bEre bEre bageya kAlika-artha koDuttadE eMdu nibaMdha tORisuttade.

### 1.7 eMcuvudu kUDa bEre bEre bAsegaLalli bEre bEre AgiruttadE {#17}

mUranesarha aDDyAyagaLa mEle badisi ee nibaMdha bAga-mUrana kEMdriya nibaMdha. baT avaru "hosa baraha" eMdu aMbuvudannu kaDeyuttAre: idu kannaD-aksaramAlEyiMda hattu mabAprANa-aksaragaLannu (Ka, Ga, Ca, Ja, Ta, Da, Ta, Da, Fa, Ba), retROphlex sibilant (Sa), mattu bEre koMDu saMskRita-eravalu padagaLige tattalAguvavE hordadu kannaDigaLalli phOnOlOjikala kArya mADada koMDu aksaragaLannu taLLedubiDuvudu. avarA vAdu: mUla kannaD padagaLu ee dvanigaLannu viSiSTavAgi upayOgisuvudilla; ee aksaragaLu kevaLa eravalu padagaLa saMskRita-barehugE uLisikOMDiruttave. aksaramAlEyannu 31–34 aksaragaLige (iMdinA 49+diMda) saraLisuvadale kalika-bArannu kaDimE mADi akSaratEyannu heccisuttade.

### 3.6 bAse mattu baraha {#36}

## bAga nAlku — kannaD bAsheya svarUpa

### 4.1 kannaDigaLa mUru mukha {#41}

kannaD kaDeEntu mUru bEre-bEre rUpagaLalli irutte: (a) dina-jIvitada mAtu-upabAse(gaLu), pradesha, jAti, talE-mArugU bErE; (b) baraha-rUpada mAnaka (grantha kannaD), sthiracitta mattu saMskRita-prabAvita; mattu (c) hala prade-Sha mattu sAmAjika upabAsegaLu (Havyaka, koDavu, guLLa, TuLu-prabAvita karaLa-kannaD, baDaga, itara). baT mUru "mukhagaLu" ella bASika-paddhatiyanalli onE-onE tyaka-samAnagaLu eMdu vAdisutte. baraha-mAnaka mattu mAtu-sAmAjika-rUpagaLa naDuvina pratiSThA-AbhEda sAmAjika utpadane, bASika satya alla.

### 4.2 kannaDigaLa upabAsegaLu {#42}

kannaDigaLa pramukha upabAse-guMpugaLa vistaRita sarvEkSaNe. baT mukhya vibhAjanagaLannu varisuttAre: karaLa upabAsegaLu (Havyaka, TuLu-prabAvita bEdagaLu), kEMdrIya puTa-upabAsegaLu, uttarA upabAsegaLu (adhika urdU/hiMdi prabAva iruva), mattu dakSiNa upabAsegaLu (adhika tamiLu prabAva iruva). upabAsegaLu bedisuvudu lakSaNagaLannu hELuttAre: pada-kOSa, ucchAraNe, mattu koMDu vyAkaraNa-lakSaNagaLu (bahuvacana-nirdharaNe). kannaDigaLa prasAranUda itihAsavannu mattu adannu koDida sAmAjika-guMpugaLannu upabAse-bEdagaLu pratibimbisutte eMbu nibaMdha okkaT TippaNi mADuttade — bASika "ahita" alla.

### 4.3 kannaDigaLa vyAkaraNa paramparE {#43}

kannaD vyAkaraNa-baREyuva itihAsAvAda sarvEkSaNe — uLidiruvalla mattu haLeyada vyAkaraNa kEsirAjara SabdamaNi darpaNa (~1260 CE) diMda hiDidu briiTiSu-kAlada vyAkaraNagaLiMda hidi 20nEya nURAdinda varege. baT ellA paramparEyu saMskRita vyAkaraNa-caukaTTinalli kAryanirvahistade eMdu tORisuttAre. kAlina kannaD vyAkaraNakAraru avara vyAkaraNagaLannu saMskRitadalli yA kannaD-saMskRita-saMkara-rUpadi baresi, spaStagoMDu pANinIya-vyAkaraNava namUne mADidarU. ee pratishThA-paramparE kannaDigaLa racaneya ShatamanasaMkhya virUpagaLannu sRiSTisidE.

### 4.4 oru nURu varSagaLa kannaD vyAkaraNa saM-sOdane {#44}

19nEya nURAdina kelaviMdA kalpitage briiTiSu aDikarArigaLu (KiTTeL, Rice), yurOpina dravIDa-jnAnigaLu (CaldveL, ZvElEbil), mattu bhaRatIya jnAnigaLu (B.D. SatyAnArAyaNa, T.V. VeNkaTacala SaSTri, mattu baT swayaM) sEridaMte kannaD bASika pAMDityannu vivisuttade. baT pratigONAde pratigONu ella aDiyali nATkiyAdadu mattu tappAdadu eMdu maulyAMkana mADuttAre, saMskRita-vargagaLu baDiyA raci-paMtaga-paddhatiyannu upayOgisida jnAnigaLu kannaDigaLa adikasariyAda vaRNanegaLannu nIDuttAre eMdu vAdisutte.

### 4.5 doDD saMskRita paMDitaru tappannu mADidAga {#45}

tIkSaNa-graMtha-kAryagaLannu biDugaDe mADida prasiddha kannaD jnAnigaLu — adhikAravaRNanAtmaka uddhAragaLannu sRiSTisidavarU — saMskRita-vargagaLannu tattariddarinda kannaD vyAkaraNavannu varisuvagala kramabaddha tappugaLannu mADiala eMdu baT tORisuttAre. janara hEsarigE vyAkaraNa-pATemadinda ellA niddEyanIdida tappugaLannu uDaharaNayoMdige koDuttAre (udA: kriyA-jODupadagaLa tappAda viSlEshaNa, vibakti-oTTugaLa tappAda vargIkaraNa). uddesha vyaktiyanu vikaTisuvudu alla; saMskRita-caukaTTu paramparEyanDika taLahake mADida saMsthAtmaka ahita tORisuvadale.

### 4.6 taMtrajnAna-padagaLa sRiSTi {#46}

vijnyAna, taMtrajnAna, mattu adhunika-jIvitakke kannaD pada-kOsavannu hEge beLesikoLLabEku? baT eraDu sAmAnya mArgagaLannu viROdhisuttAre: (1) saMskRita-mUla nava-padagaLu guNaprAya-kaDeyuttuvudu (udA: dUradarSana "television"), anupRavEshyavAda mattu prAptyasADyavAda padagaLannu sRiSTisuttade; mattu (2) iMgliSu padagaLannu samAgra-rUpadi anusarisu, iMgliSu-jnAna illAdavarannu (non-English speakers) hERE biDuttade. mUranesarha mArgakke battisuttAre: kannaDigaLa svanta utpAdaka mUla-oTTugaLiMda hosa padagaLannu cekkugUdisuvadale (*heserike* "naming/nomenclature", *nuDi-arime* "linguistics", itara). nivaMdha avara swantada SifAriSu-padagaLannu sErisuttade.

## bAga aidu — jagattina bAsegaLu

### 5.1 bAratada bAsegaLu {#51}

bArata jagattina adika bASika-vividhate iruva desha, 700 hEccu bAsegaLonDige. baT nAlku mukhya bArata-bAse-kuTuMbagaLannu sarvEkSisuttAre: dravIDa (kannaD, tamiLu, teLugu, maLALam, mattu halavAru cinna bAsegaLu), iMDO-AriyA (hiMdi, baMgALi, marATi, gujarAti, itara), TibetO-burma (maNipuri, boDO, mattu EsAnE bhaRatada hala bAsegaLu), mattu AusTrO-eshiyAtika (saMtAli, muMDAri, khAsi). "bASika kSEtra" (India samAgrata: retroflexion, SOV krama, eravalu-pada-kOsa) viSayavanu vivEcisuttAre. "hiMdi bAratada "rASTrabhASA"" eMba mithyeyannu viROdhisuttAre — adu kevaLa sumbAru 40% janarige tAy-nuDi.

### 5.2 Esiyada bAsegaLu {#52}

Esiyada mukhya bAse-kuTuMbagaLa sarvEkSaNe: SinO-TibeTan (cIni, TibeTan, birma), JapOnik (jApAni), kOrEAN, Altaik (tarkI, maMgOliyA, mAMcu), sEmITik (arabi, hibrU), IrAniyA (phArsi, pashTO), mattu ekakulA (jApAni, kOrIyan). Esiya jagattinalli adhikajalanu mAtanADuvavara bAsegaLannu (maMDarIna cIni, hiMdi, arabi) mattu racane-rItyalli adika viShESavAda koMDu bAsegaLannu (jApAni, adara viSEdavAda gauRava-vyavasthE mattu viSaya-pratikRiyA-racane) hoDagisuttade eMdu baT TippaNi mADuttAre.

### 5.3 yurOpina bAsegaLu {#53}

iMDO-yurOpina kuTuMba yurOpinalli pramukha, adara mukhya ShAkhegaLiMdige: jarmAniya (iMgliSu, jarmanu, DaTcu, svEDiSu), rOmAnsu (phreNcu, spEniSu, iTeliyana, pOrTugIzu, rOmAniyana), SlavIya (raSiyA, pOliSu, cEku), mattu bErE (grIku, albEniyA, ArmEniyA). yurOpina ekakulA bAsegaLannu TippaNi mADuttAre: bAsku (ekakulA), phiMniSu mattu eSTOniyana (yurAliku). nijavaagi grIku mattu lATinu yurOpinalli SastrIya bAsegaLAgi (dakSiNa Esiyalli saMskRita kArya nirvahisuvaMtE) pAtRa nirvahistave eMdU, iMgliSu jAgatika saMparka-bAsEyAgi pracalavalidu eMdU nibaMdha hELuttade.

### 5.4 Aprikada bAsegaLu {#54}

Aprika jagattinA adhika bASika-vividhate iruva khaND, 2,000 hEccu bAsegaLonDige. baT nAlku mukhya Aprika kuTuMbagaLannu sarvEkSisuttAre: Nijer-kaMgO (adariMda adhikabalagu doDD, svAhili, zuLu, yOruba sEridaMte), NilO-sAhARA, Aprō-eshiyAtika (arabi, amhArik, hAusA), mattu kOisAna (adara viSiSTavAda kliku-dvanigaLonDige). Aprikada UDAdina yurOpiya bAsegaLannu sarkArI-dajE-gE ettida kaLaniya-itihAsavannu, idu ShikSaNa mattu akSaratEge udayavAguva bAdegaLannu, mattu Aprika bAse-samudhAyagaLa nittyanAda chaitanyavannu nibaMdha vivisuttade.

### 5.5 amErikada bAsegaLu {#55}

amErikagaLa svadEsha bAsegaLu halavAru bEre bEre bAse-kuTuMbagaLannu pratiNidhisutte — amErikagaLa modalina janAvasEyA naMtara 15,000+ varSagaLa bEre bEre beLavaNigeya phala. mukhya guMpugaLannu (Algonkina, AThabAskan, OTO-maMgiyA, kEcuA, TupiyA) vivEcisi, yurOpiya vasahatiya kAraNavAgi bASika-nAsu aTTu kaSTakara AgiddE eMdu TippaNi mADuttAre. spEniSu, pOrTugIzu, iMgliSu iMdu rAjakIyavAgi pramukha Adare hala swadESa bAsegaLu — kEcuA (80 lakSa mAtanADuvavaru), guarAni (50 lakSa), NAhuATalu (15 lakSa) — balavanThavAgi mAtanADalpaDuttave.

### 5.6 AsTrEliyada bAsegaLu {#56}

AsTrEliya AbOrijinaL bAsegaLu (hala nURu) oMdu bEre bEre bAse-kuTuMba-sRiNikhalEyannu (pAma-nyungana khaND-vAgE pramukha, uttarAdalli bEre bEre sambaMdhitAlla kuTuMbagaLu). baT vasahatiya kAraNavirali hala AsTrEliya bAsegaLu inDu apAya-sthitiyalli yA nAshavAgiddE eMdu TippaNi mADuttAre. ### 5.7 OsiyAniyAda bAsegaLu {#57}

AusTrOnESiya kuTuMba, pOlEnESiya (hAvAiyan, MAOri, sAmOa), MElAnESiya, mattu mikrOnESiyA bAsegaLannu hoDagisuttade, madagAskara (Aprika kiNcAnE) diMda hAvAi (prashAMta mahAsAgara) varege — yAva bAse-kuTuMbadinAdarU adhika bhaumika-praSArE. ee viSaya purAtana AusTrOnESiya samudra-yAtrigaLu ee bAsegaLannu prashAMta-mahAsAgaravirali koDida eMbadannu hELuttade. papuA nyU gini mattu kUDa hELuttAre, jagattinalli adhika bAse-santate iruva desha: ondu deShadalli 800 hEccu bAsegaLu.

### 5.8 muktAya {#58}

pustaka modalina viSayakke hOguttade: sAmAnya janaru tiLidukOMDiguvudiginta bAsheya bagge kaMmi tiLidiruttAre, avaru hoDagisiruva tappudAri-grahahagaLu nijavaAgi parIhara-bAdavAda pariNAmagaLannu hoDagisuttave. bAsheyannu vaisnikavAgi tiLidukOLLuvudu — yAvu bAse mElu alla eMbu, ellA bAsegaLu badalaAguttave eMbu, baraha mAtinaDiyalli baruttade eMbu, vyAkaraNa-vividhate mAnava-saMpaTTu eMbu — kannaDigaLu tama bAsheya bagge, karnATakadalli bASika alpasaMkhyAtara bagge, mattu jagattinalli kannaDigaLa sthAnada bagge hEge cintisuttAre eMbadannu badalaAgabEku. pustaka hEgE mugidate badalAvisuvudu: maMdagana bhAvana-cALuvike, polemical kArya-parikalpaneyonDige alla; naru kaMDupidi: adigeDe bAsheya bagge spaSTagoMDu hELikE tiLiduttade eMdu bhAvisuvAgala, nuDiyarimeyavannu oMdE iru-sAri parizIlisuMTe eMdu sahaja-nimaMtraNa.

## mULa transcript: sImita mattu sambhaLa

I pustakada YouTube udanga transcript bahaLa maNintu svalpa labhyavAgiruvu. kAraNa:

- **bhAga 1:** transcript labhyavilla ennuva cihne iddare-U append-ADa paThya bErE series-ninda tiLisuttade — sarvAnAma (pronoun) viShayada vyAkaraNa nAlku. baT-ra lEkhana alla.
- **bhAga 2:** transcript pUrNa labhyavilla.
- **bhAga 3:** oDAnavanAda transcript — hELuvavaru baT-ra palabra racane pustakavanu muKyastuti mADisuttAre.
- **bhAga 4:** bhAga 1 paThyadE — verbatim — puna-hAjirAGiruvu. bErE video noDeyitu.

**I pustaka baT baredadu alla** — hELuvavaru baT-ra kAryadannu munnaLegaru kannaDa bhAShAarThe hELuttAre. "dArege doDDavaru" enna Sirashike — "kannaDa dAriyalli doDDa kElasa mADigiruva mahAjanaru" — baT-ra kavanaka seremoni adadenno gothDilla.

## viShaya 2 — bhAga 3: baT-ra palabra racane pustakada stuti

*bhAga 3 mAtra*

[📼 bhAga 3 →](../kn/full#part-3)

bhAga 3-nalli oDAnavanAda paThya:

hELuvavaru baT-ra palabra racane pustakada kaDe idannu hELuttAre: kannaDa mattu ingliShu padagaLu kannaDakke bandAga "bahalA badalavAgi vikasavAgi mArpATAgive". A pustakadalli kannaDa vAkya racane mattu "kannaDadalli artha hEge kalpaneyAgide" ennuvudu racane mADigide. samskxta vyAkaraNa niyamagaLannu nirakarisuttave — kannaDada svantada niyamagaLannu nAmapada, sarvAnAma, kriyApada, guNapada ivugaLige sthApaneyAgide.

A pustakavanu hELuvavanu "krAntikarI" (revolutionary) mattu "atyanta mahattvapoNDa" (supremely important) ennuttAre. karnATaka SikShaNa ilAKe mattu prAthamika shALe, kAlEjugaLu — ellAdakkU avaSyavAda pustaka ennuttAre. "saMpUrNa ingliShu anuvAda" ennuva mattu — adEna ennuvudu niscita alla; Book 32 adadenno sambandha iruvu.

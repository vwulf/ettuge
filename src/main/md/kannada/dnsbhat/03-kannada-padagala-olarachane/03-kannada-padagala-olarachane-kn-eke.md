## 1. pITike {#pITike}

### 1.1 oLaracane (ಒಳರಚನೆ)

kannaDigaLali eraDu bageya padagaLu idave: oLaracane iruva padagaLu mattu oLaracane illada padagaLu. "munnuDi" eMba padavu "muMde" mattu "nuDi" eMba eraDu padagaLu sEridagilive — ide adu oLaracane iruva pada. "tale" eMba padavu oLaracane illada pada; adanu muMde "tala" mattu innoMdu pada eMdu bida-bida mADalAguvudilla. "kuNita" eMba pada "kuNi" (verb) mattu "ta" (hesaravAgu mADuva prattaya) eMdu bidu-bidu mADalAguvudu. oLaracane ariyadE pada upayOgisabahudu, Adare oLaracane ariyadiddare hosa padagaLa artha hoMde terakOLLuvudu, hosa padagaLannu kaDedabiDuvudu, mattu anuvAdakke sahAyavAguvudu.

### 1.2 oLaracane mattu carittre (ಒಳರಚನೆ ಮತ್ತು ಚರಿತ್ರೆ)

padada oLaracane mattu adara carittre ondu allada eraDu biMdugaLu. oru jODupada modalige sEridAga, adara racane mattu utpatti ondu. Adare kALakramadi padagaLu badalAguttave: koMDu aM-sagaLu svataMindriya artha kaLedukOMDu kevaLa ottu (suffix) Aguttave; itoraMDu aM-sagaLu dvani-badalavalinda mUla padagaLa sambaMda kANisuvudillava. "eNNe" eMba pada carittre-driSTiyalli "eLLu" (sesame) mattu "ney" (teL, fat) inda bandide, Adare iMdinA kannaDigaLali adu oLaracane illada ondu pada. baT avaru samakAlIna dRiSTi (synchronic) mattu carittre-dRiSTi (historical) eMdu eraDu arikattu bEre bEre eMdu tORisuttAre.

### 1.3 padagaLu mattu padarUpagaLu (ಪದಗಳು ಮತ್ತು ಪದರೂಪಗಳು)

"baD mudukanannu, rAju bisinIrinalli mellage muLugisida" eMba vAkyadi pada mattu padarUpa eMba vIrupadannu kANabahudAda. "muduka" eMba pada, "mudukanannu" eMba padarUpadi kANisuttade — idali "annu" eMba vibakti oTTu sEruttade. "nIru" eMba pada "nIrinalli" eMba padarUpadi kANisuttade. hIge vibakti oTTugaLu padarUpagaLannu sRiSTisuttave; idu pada-sRiSTi mADuva payadoTTugaLa kArya alla. ee eraDu bageya oTTugaLa (lexical vs inflectional) vIrupadavu ee pustakadinaduva aDigAdi tattva.

### 1.4 padagaLu mattu oTTugaLu (ಪದಗಳು ಮತ್ತು ಒಟ್ಟುಗಳು)

oMdu jODupadada aMsu swataMindriya-pada eMdare, adu bErE kaDeyalli svataMindriya-pada Agi bAruvudu sADya: "kAlmaNe" padadi modalina aMsu "kAl" idu svataMindriya pada "kAlu" (foot) inda bandide, hIgAgi eraDU aMsugaLU padagaLe. "kuNita" dalli "ta" eMba aMsu svataMindriya-pada Agi bAruvudilla — adu oTTu. koMDu aMsugaLu — "hAray" eMba aMsu "hAraysu" ([[ε→]] look/cause to see) dalli — pada alla, oTTu alla, aMtarake illada pada (horakuLi pada / bound root) eMdu keLabahudAdu.

### 1.5–1.8 padavoTTu mattu padarUpada oTTu; utpAdaka racane (ಪದದೊಟ್ಟು ಮತ್ತು ಪದರೂಪದೊಟ್ಟು)

1.5–1.7 kaMDagaLu padavoTTu (lexical affix) mattu padarUpada oTTu (inflectional affix) eMda eraDara naDuvina aRu bedagaLannu koDuttave: (a) padavoTTu hosa pada kaDeyuttade, anaivArya alla; padarUpada oTTu anivArya. (b) padavoTTu kelavu padagaLige mAtra tattaruttade; padarUpada oTTu ellA padagaLige tattaruttade. (c) padavoTTu paritigaLannu mADuvudilla; padarUpada oTTu paritigaLannu (paradigm) mADuttade. (d) padavoTTannu bErE nuDigaLinda eravalu mADabahudu; padarUpada oTTannu eravalu mADuvudilla. (e) padavoTTige ekavIDa artha iruvadittu; padarUpada oTTige sthira artha irutte. (f) padavoTTu eravalu padagaLige tattaruvudilla sADAraNatah. 1.7 kaMDavu utpAdaka mattu anutpAdaka racane-vIrupadannu vivarisi, 1.8 idu kannaDigaLa svalpa (Dravidian-native) padagaLa arikattu upayOgisi kannaD vyAkaraNa barevudAgide eMdu hELuttade.

## 2. kannaDigaLa pada-guMpugaLu {#pada-gumpugaLu}

*(ಕನ್ನಡದ ಪದಗುಂಪುಗಳು)*

### 2.1 muMnOTa (ಮುನ್ನೋಟ)

ee aDDyAya kannaDigaLa mUru bageya pada-guMpugaLannu sthApisi, moMdina aDDyAyagaLige nele haradi: hesaru pada (ಹೆಸರುಪದ, noun), esaka pada (ಎಸಕಪದ, verb), parice pada (ಪರಿಚೆಪದ, qualifier/adjective). ee mUru guMpugaLu kannaD vAkyadi mUru bEre bEre kArya nirvahisuttave: hesaru padagaLu vyakti, vastugaLu, nele, mattu biMdugaLannu gurtisuttave; esaka padagaLu kAryagaLannu yA nelegaLannu prAti-gattisuttave; parice padagaLu hesaru padagaLa dharmagaLannu varisuttave.

### 2.2 pada-guMpugaLalli vyatyAsagaLu (ಪದಗುಂಪುಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ವ್ಯತ್ಯಾಸಗಳು)

nuDigaLu pada-guMpugaLannu hEge vargIsuttave eMbude bEre bEre. dravIDa, iMDO-AriyA, tibe-barma, AsTrO-eshiyA eMba nAlku nADina nAlku nuDi-kuTuMbagaLu pada-guMpugaLannu bEre bEre rIti-yalli vibhajisuttave. saMskRitadi svarUpadaDi baLasudE bEre guNa-pada (adjective) varga iruvadittu alla — saMskRit guNa-padagaLu viBakti, linga, vacana eMdella hesaru-pada-samAge baddalAguttave. kannaD parice padagaLu, iMtAhikke, vibakti yA vacanakke baddalAguvudilla.

### 2.3 pada-guMpugaLa mUru bEre guNagaLu (ಪರಿಚೆಧರ್ಮಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ವ್ಯತ್ಯಾಸ)

ee kaMDavu aDDyAyada mAhitivina mOLe. baT avaru eDu bEre bEre vivEcanA parIkSegaLannu koDuttAre: (1) nEraLa upayOga: parice padagaLu mattu hesaru padagaLu nEraLakke hesaru-pada muMdE bandu varisi biDabahudu; esaka padagaLige idu sADya alla. (2) artha-bedagaLu: hesaru padagaLu vyakti-uddesha mADuttave; parice padagaLu niMgaDa svalpa dharma varisuttave. (3) hOlikeyalli: parice padagaLu mAtra hOlikeyalli bALuttave (eg: "idu A manegiMta doDD mane"). (4) vismaya-vAkyadi: "eSTu doDD mane!" eMballi mAtra parice padagaLu bALuttave. (5) mArdani-pada sRiSTiyalli: mArdani padagaLannu (echo words) hesaru mattu esaka padagaLinda mADabahudu, parice padagaLinda alla. (6) svataMindriya upayOgadi: parice padagaLu hesaru mattu esaka padagaLa hAge svataMindriya yAgi bALuvudilla. (7) sthira vs. cala dharmagaLu: parice padagaLu sthira dharmagaLannu varisuttave; esaka padagaLu acala/cAra-kAlIna nelegaLannu hELuttave.

### 2.4–2.5 pada-guMpu sadasyatva mattu sArAMsa

keMDagaLu 2.4–2.5 hesarisuttade: koMDu padagaLu eraDu guMpugaLa naDuvE iddarU iruttave; pada-guMpu-sadasyatva saMpUrNa sthira alla. Adare mUru-vibhajana kannaD vyAkaraNakke AdhAra-bhUta mattu aDDyAyagaLalli hOguvudakke anaivArya eMdu aDDyAya mugisutte.

## 3. hesaru padagaLa oLaracane {#hesaru-padagaLa-oLaracane}

*(ನಾಮಪದಗಳ ಒಳರಚನೆ)*

kannaD hesaru padagaLu eraDu kArya mADuttave: vyaktigaLannu gurtisu (hEsarisu) yA avara dharmagaLannu vari (varisu). aDDyAya modalige ee eraDu kArya beda-beda eMdu tORisi, naMtara jODupada mattu hesaru-pada-kaMte (noun phrase) naDuvina bedavannu sparSisuttade. jODupada (ಜೋಡುಪದ) ondu svalpa padiMdIya artha iruva pada, aMte; hesaru-pada-kaMte (ಹೆಸರುಪದ ಕಂತೆ) vyAkaraNavalla sERisida artha-kaMte.

jODupada mattu hesaru-pada-kaMte naDuvina bedannu gurtisalu, baT avaru Edu vivEcanA parIkSegaLannu koDuttAre: (1) utpAdakata — hosa kaMteyanu swEcChegE sRiSTisabahuduvo yA kevaLa smRiti-pada Agi iDabahuduvo? (2) dharma-aD-ike — jODupadadi aMsugaLa yAvu dharmagaLu uLidiruttave? (3) artha-viparyAsa — jODupada mUla aMsugaLa kuDAmi-arthadinda bEre artha paDeyuttadeyE? (4) vyApti-mIti — aMsugaLannu bEre bEre mArgadi parisarisi biDabahuduvo? (5) dvani-badala — aMsugaLu sEruvAga saMdhi yA dvani-badala Aguttadeyē? (6) krama-mIti — aMsugaLu bEre kramadi bAruvududu sADya-vE? (7) hesaru-pada jODupada mattu hesaru-pada-hesaru-pada-kaMte naDuvina saMbaMdha.

aDDyAya rUDhanAma (proper nouns) mattu aMkitanAma (common nouns) naDuvina bedavannu kaNDukoLLuttade — rUDhanAma parimANa mattu niScarita-artha-vivEcaneyalli bEre dharmagaLannu tORisuttade.

## 4. prattayaviruva hesaru padagaLu {#prattayaviruva-hesaru-padagaLu}

*(ಪ್ರತ್ಯಯವಿರುವ ನಾಮಪದಗಳು)*

ee aDDyAya pustakadinaduva maha-vistAradadu. idu esaka padagaLinda hesaru padagaLannu, hesaru padagaLinda hesaru padagaLannu, parice padagaLinda hesaru padagaLannu tOLeda utpAdaka padavoTTugaLannu vistAravAgi vargIsuttade. keMDa 4.3 adalli paTTi 10 oTTugaLannu vivarisi esaka-mUlinda hesaru padagaLannu tOLedukOLLuttade:

- **-ike/-ige (ಇಕೆ/ಇಗೆ):** ee oTTu kannaDigaLali adhika utpAdaka mattu anek-artha. kArya-hesarugaLannu (hogaLike "praise"), pariNAma-hesarugaLannu (kettike "carving"), sAdhana/vastu-hesarugaLannu (moLake "sprout") sRiSTisuttade. ee oTTu koMDu vivEcaneyalli vibakti oTTAgiyu bALuttade.
- **-ta/-t (ತ/ತ್):** kArya-hesarugaLannu sRiSTisuttade (kuNita "dancing", nageta "laughter").
- **-a (ಅ):** tirugatiruga eMbatti esaka padagaLinda mElu abstrakTa hesarugaLannu sRiSTisuttade.
- **-vu (ವು):** purAtana yA mIti-sAdhya hesaru-tOLedu-oTTu.
- **-vaLi (ವಳಿ):** adu mADuva vyakti yA karma-karta.
- **-vaN (ವಣ್):** rIti yA svabhAva.
- **-e (ಎ):** esaka-mUlinda hesarugaLannu sRiSTisuttade.
- **-alu (ಅಲು):** sAdhana yA kArya-hesarugaLannu sRiSTisuttade (meTTalu "stair").
- **-ku (ಕು):** esaka-mUlinda hesarugaLannu sRiSTisuttade.
- **sonnye prattaya (ಸೊನ್ನೆ ಪ್ರತ್ಯಯ):** kannaDigaLa hala hesaru padagaLu spaSTagoMDa oTTu illadeyE esaka-mUlinda sRiSTi Aguttave — esaka-mUle svayamE hesaru Aguttade (nagu "laughter/to laugh", ODu "running/to run").

keMDa 4.4 hesaru-padiMda hesaru-pada-sRiSTi (noun-from-noun derivation) vivarisi, 4.5 parice-padiMda hesaru-pada-sRiSTiyannu, nEraLa upayOga mattu oTTu-saha-rUpagaLannu sparSisuttade.

## 5. jODupadagaLu {#jODupadagaLu}

*(ಜೋಡುಪದಗಳು)*

ee aDDyAya kannaDigaLalli jODike (compounding) eMba vistAradavAda ciMtane mADuttade. modalina keMDagaLu (5.1–5.3) jODupada mattu ee mUru viSayagaLa naDuvina viparyAsagaLannu sparSisuttave: (a) kevaLa eraDu padagaLannu jODisida kaMtegaLu, (b) prattaya iruva padagaLu, mattu (c) mAlu-nuDi/dvandvagaLu. aDDyAya naMtara nAlku vargIkaraNa mAnagaLannu koDuttade.

aMsagaLa saMkhyeyinda vargIkaraNa (5.6.1): kannaD jODupadagaLu eraDu-aMsagaLavAgirutte (binary). modalina aMsada pada-guMpininda vargIkaraNa (5.6.2): ee adika mukyavAda mAna, mUru mukhya bageya jODupadagaLu sRiSTAvAguttave: (a) hesaru + hesaru yA hesaru + esaka (modalina aMsu hesaru pada), (b) esaka + hesaru yA esaka + esaka (modalina aMsu esaka pada), mattu (c) parice + hesaru (modalina aMsu parice pada). eraDaneyada aMsada vividhateyinda vargIkaraNa (5.6.3). "kEMdra-biMdu" (head) sthAnada vargIkaraNa (5.6.4): kannaD jODupadagaLalli kEMdra-biMdu kaDeya aMsu Agirabahudu (right-headed), Adare koMDu jODupadagaLu "bahirstha" (exocentric) Agiruttave — kEMdra-biMdu eraDU aMsugaLAgiruvadittu.

keMDa 5.7 padagaLu jODuvAga jaraguva dvani-badala-gOnnu vivisuttade: eraDaneyada padakkAguvudu (5.7.1) mattu modalina padakkAguvudu (5.7.2). keMDa 5.8 saMskRita-samAsa-vargIkaraNe (tatpuruSa, dvaMdva, bahuvrIhi, avyayibhAva, karmadhaAraya) kannaD jODupadagaLige hEge tattalAguvudilla mattu hEge kannaD vAstavadannu virUpagOLisuttade eMdu vimarsa-saha vivEcisuttade.

## 6. kriyA padagaLa oLaracane {#kriyA-padagaLa-oLaracane}

*(ಕ್ರಿಯಾಪದಗಳ ಒಳರಚನೆ)*

kannaD esaka padagaLu hesaru padagaLigiMta rachane-driSTiyalli bEre. adhika prAthamika esaka padagaLu oLaracane illada mUla-rUpagaLe. aDDyAya 6.1 esaka padagaLa kArya-yA-nele-sRiSTiyannu vivarisi, avukke bALatanaRivu (agent, patient, location, time) bEkAguttade eMdu hELuttade. saMskRita "kriyasamAsa" eMba tatta kannaDigaLige hEge mIti-tattalAguttade eMdU koMDu gantavyannu tORisuttade.

keMDa 6.2 oLaracane-illada esaka padagaLannu (simplex verbs) varisuttade — iMdinA kannaDigaLalli pratyekaLa utpatti kANada mUla-rUpagaLu. keMDa 6.3 purAtana oTTugaLannu (fossil affixes) vivarisi, oMde kArya mADuttiddAga -cu aMtAha oTTugaLu iMdu utpAdaka alladannu hELuttade. keMDa 6.4 sAdyava utpAdaka -isu aMtAha kAraka-oTTannu varisuttade — antaKkriyA esaka padagaLinda kAraka-esaka padagaLannu sRiSTisuttade (muLugu "to sink" → muLugisu "to cause to sink"). keMDa 6.5 kUDupadagaLannu (ಕೂಡುಪದಗಳು / verbal compounds) vivarisi, hesaru yA parice aMsagaLu esaka padadi sEraganuvagina aDDyAya-5 da jODupadagaLigiMta bEre rItiyalli artha-sRiSTi mADuttave eMdu tORisuttade.

## 7. guNa padagaLa oLaracane {#guNa-padagaLa-oLaracane}

*(ಗುಣಪದಗಳ ಒಳರಚನೆ)*

parice padagaLu (ಪರಿಚೆಪದ / ಗುಣಪದ) mUru mukhya pada-guMpugaLalli adika saraLavAda oLaracane hondivave. kannaD parice padagaLu prAthamika (mUla, sRiSTA-illada) Agirabahudu — biLi (white), doDD (big), oLLe (good) aMtAhavugaLu — yA hesaru mattu esaka padagaLinda svalpa-prattayagaLa mUlaka sRiSTi Agirabahudu. keMDa 7.1 kannaD parice padagaLu linga, vacana, vibaktikkAgE baddalAguvadittu eMdu mukyavAda tatta-dRiSTiyalli tORisuttade — idu saMskRita guNa-padagaLigiMta pramukha bedavAda viSaya.

keMDa 7.2 parice pada upa-bheTagaLa naDuvina sUkShma bedagaLannu — sthira dharmagaLannu varisuvavugaLu (kappu "black") mattu hEra-krama-saMbaMdhita dharmagaLannu varisuvavugaLu naDuvina bedannu — vivarisi. keMDa 7.3 parice padagaLu tALuva mUla rUpagaLannu varisuttade: hala ekamAtravAgide; koMDu mAlu-nuDigaLAgide; koMDu hesaru padagaLinda -a yA bErE oTTu sERisidiruttave. keMDa 7.4 bErE pada-guMpugaLinda parice padagaLannu sRiSTisalu upayOgavAguvudu dERa svalpa oTTugaLannu — hesaru-sRiSTi oTTugaLa saMkhyeyiMta adika kaDime eMdu — vivarsi.

## 8. eravalu padagaLa oLaracane {#eravalu-padagaLa-oLaracane}

*(ಎರವಲಾಗಿ ಬಂದ ಪದಗಳ ಒಳರಚನೆ)*

ee aDDyAya bErE nuDigaLinda, mukhyavAgi saMskRitiMda, kannaDigaLige eravalu baMda padagaLannu sparSisuttade. keMDa 8.1 eravalu (borrowing) sAmAnya vidiMdamannu vivEcisi: bEre bEre bageya eravalugaLannu (8.1.1) vivarisi, tatsama padagaLu (saMskRita-mUla dvani-badala illa upayogi padagaLu) mattu tadbava padagaLu (saMskRita-mUla dvani-saMpUrNa kannaDigaLIkaraNa padagaLu) naDuvina bedannu (8.1.2) hELi, eravalu padagaLalli dvani-badalakkAguvudu yAkE eMbu (8.1.3) vivarisi, eravalu mADuvAga pada-guMpu saDasyatva hEge baddalAgutte eMdU (8.1.4) sparSisuttade.

keMDa 8.2 eravalu padagaLa oLaracane vivarisi: (8.2.1) avara oLaracane native kannaD padagaLa oLaracaneyiMta hEge bEre Agirabahudu, (8.2.2) mUla-nuDiya vyAkaraNa-niyama eravalu-rUpada oLagE hEge uLidiruttade, mattu (8.2.3) kannaD-native vivEcanA-mAnagaLu eravalu padagaLannu hEge vAyuttave eMbadannu. keMDa 8.3 viSeSatah saMskRita eravalugannu keLibaM: (8.3.1) saMskRita mattu kannaD upayOgadi ondu mUlada bEre pada-guMpu sadasyatva, (8.3.2) saMskRita muMnoTTugaLu mattu prattayagaLu eravaluMdi kannaDigaLige senDuvudu, (8.3.3) esaka-mUlada bedagaLu, mattu (8.3.4) eravalu saMskRita prattayagaLa kannaDigaLalli utpAdakata. keMDagaLu 8.4–8.6 eravalu prattaya-pada (muMnoTTu 8.4.1, prattaya 8.4.2, dvani-badala 8.4.3), eravalu esaka pada (8.5), mattu eravalu jODupada (8.6) sparSisuttade.

## 9. muktAya {#muktAya}

*(ಮುಕ್ತಾಯ)*

muktAya aDDyAya pustakadinaduva mukhya tIrmAnagaLannu sErisuttade mattu kannaD-native vyAkaraNa-parampareyanu kaTTalu naDeyuva doDDa yOjaneyalli idu elli niMtu eMbadannu dORisuttade. baT avaru hEge pratiyu pada-guMpu — hesaru padagaLu, esaka padagaLu, parice padagaLu — avara svanta raci-vyavasthe, avara svanta utpAdaka prattayagaLu, avara svanta jODike-vicAragaLu hoMdivave eMdu sArAMsa mADuttAre. hesaru pada-racaneyalli (aDDyAyagaLu 3–5) vividhatA mattu utpAdakata parice-pada-racaneyalli (aDDyAya 7) mattu kArana-prErita esaka-pada-racaneyalli (aDDyAya 6) adika saraLavAda racanegiMta virodhISAhavAda vyatyAsavannu tORisuttade.

eraDu sahasrama varSagaLu kannaD vyAkaraNakAraru saMskRita-vyAkaraNa sAdhana-gArannu (pANinIya lOpa, AgAma, AdEsa; samAsa-vargIkaraNe; kAraka-paddhati) upayogi-mADuttA bandidare. ee sAdhana-gAru, baT avaru vAdisuvarente, kannaD hEge naDeyuttade eMbadale vyavasthita virUpagaLannu sRiSTisiddave. ee pustakadinaduva kaDedabiTTa kannaD-native oLaracane-vicAra — oTTu, padoTTu, padarUpadoTTu, jODupada, hesaru pada / esaka pada / parice pada aMta svalpa parigaLannu upayOgisi — dravIDiya vaRNanAtmaka nuDi-arikattu (descriptive linguistics) kaDe ondu adaMgU. pustaka biblio-graphy mattu viSaya-nirdeshadoMdige mugiyuttade.

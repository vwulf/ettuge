## adhyAya ೯ — ADupadagaLu

[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi) | [ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#adhyAya-9)

nAnu, nInu, mattu tAnu — I mUru padagaLannu 'ADupadagaLu' emba bEreya gurUpinalli sErisal Aguttade. avugaLu sollinalli bandu tamma Aduge, kELuge, ilvE bErobbara samba nDavannu tiLisuttave. solla nirmisuvalli ivugaLa kArya viceShTavAgiruttade.

<a id="sec-9-1"></a>

#### 9.1 munnOTa

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sec-9-1)

<a id="sub-9-1-1"></a>

##### 9.1.1 ADupadagaLa kelasa

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-1-1)

nAnu, nInu, tAnu emba mUru padagaLu sollinallli tamma AdagE Adu kArya naDesuttave — sollinalli bandu oru pAngu Aduge, kELuge, ilvE bErobbara embudu tiLisuvudu. (1k) nAnu UnTa mADuttiddEne — illi 'nAnu' pAngu Aduge embudu tiLisuttade, A Aduge yAru endalla.

<a id="sub-9-1-2"></a>

##### 9.1.2 tAnu padada bErpaDuge

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-1-2)

tAnu padavu nAnu-nInu padagaLinda bErevAgi bErobbara pAngu Aduge ilvE kELuga allada emba aLLagaLeya huruLannu koDuttade. udA: tanage nOvAdaru rAju mAtanADuvudilla — illi 'tanage' pAngu Aduge ilvE kELuga alla. I bErpaDuge tAnunnu viceShTa pada mADuttade.

<a id="sub-9-1-3"></a>

##### 9.1.3 hesarupadagaLinda bErAgiruvudu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-1-3)

ADupadagaLu hesarupadagaLantalla — avugaLu yAra hesarannu sUcisuttave embudu sollinalli yAru mAtanADidaru enbadannu nODi tiLidukoLLabEku. rAju, madhu hAge niccita hesaru koDuvudilla; nAnu 'Aduge' embudu mAtrana koDuttade.

<a id="sub-9-1-4"></a>

##### 9.1.4 tOrUpadagaLinda bErAgiruvudu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-1-4)

ADupadagaLu tOrUpadagaLantalla. avanu, avaLu emba tOrUpadagaLu yAradaru obbara hesarannu sUcisabahudAdarU avugaLa kElasa viceShTavAgi tOrkey a nIDisuvudu. ADupadagaLu mAtrana samba ndada sUcane koDuttave — tOrkey a alla.

<a id="sub-9-1-5"></a>

##### 9.1.5 sollannu unTumADuvalli bagegaLu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-1-5)

ADupadagaLa baLakeyinda sollannu nirmisuvalli mUru bagegaLu kANabAruttave: (1) nAnu/nInu pAngAgi baruva sollu, (2) tAnu pAngAgi baruva sollu, (3) bahuvacanada ADupadagaLu (nAvu, nIvu, tAvu) pAngAgi baruva sollu. pratyEka bageyU solla rUpadalli viceShTa lacaNagaLannu tOruttade.

#### 9.2 ADupadagaLa kelasa

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sec-9-2)

ADupadagaLu sollinalli 'mArikeyada padagaLu' (substitutes) Agi kelasagaiyuttave — avugaLu solla nirmanada samparkadalli Aduge-kELugara samba ndavannu tiLisuttave. avugaLa baLake solladalli mArpADugaLige kAraNaAguva kelavu lacaNagaLannu I bAgadalli vidarisi.

<a id="sub-9-2-1"></a>

##### 9.2.1 mArikeyada padagaLu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-1)

ADupadagaLu hesarupadagaLa mArike mADisuttave enbA nambike sariyalla — avugaLu hesarannu sUcisuvudilla, kelasannu (Aduge/kELuge emba samba ndavannu) sUcisuttave. (2k) rAju ninage oru pustaka koDaliddAne — illi 'ninage' pAngu kELuge embudu tiLisuttade.

<a id="sub-9-2-2"></a>

##### 9.2.2 pAngugaLannu gurutisalu nEravallada holabu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-2)

ADupadagaLu sollinalli pAngugaLannu nEraDAgi guruti sade nEravallada (indirect) holabininda guruti suttave. udA: nAnu padavannu nODidare A sollinalli mADuga pAngu Aduge embudu tiLiyuttade, yAru endalla.

<a id="sub-9-2-3"></a>

##### 9.2.3 horapaDikeyAgi madipu baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-3)

ADupadagaLu madipu oTTinondige bandare (nAnadrU, nInadrU) avugaLa sAmAnya kElasa mArpaTTaguttade — horapaDikeyAgi (exception-marker Agi) kelasagaiyuttave.

<a id="sub-9-2-4"></a>

##### 9.2.4 ettuge sollugaLalli baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-4)

ADupadagaLu ettuge sollugaLalli (quotative clauses — endu bandu oLasollugaLalli) viceShTa lacaNagaLannu tOruttave. oLasollinalli bandu nAnu, nInu enbu padagaLu oLasolla nirmanisida Aduge-kELugarannu sUcisuttave.

<a id="sub-9-2-5"></a>

##### 9.2.5 endu padada baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-5)

'endu' pada (quotative complementizer) bayasuvudu, nambuvudu, hELuvudu hAge esaka padagaLondhige baLakeyAguva oLasollugaLannu joDisuttade. I oLasollugaLalli bandu ADupadagaLu oLasolla nirmanisida Aduge-kELugarannu nirdEsi suttave.

<a id="sub-9-2-6"></a>

##### 9.2.6 bEre mArpADugaLu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-6)

ADupadagaLu bandAga solladalli innaSTu mArpADugaLu tOruttave — esaka padada aLLagaLe rUpagaLu, pattuge rUpagaLalli vyatyAsagaLu, halavaNumba rUpagaLu.

<a id="sub-9-2-7"></a>

##### 9.2.7 ante padada baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-2-7)

'ante' pada (reportative/hearsay marker) ADupadagaLondhige sEridAga viceShTa lacaNagaLannu tOruttade. 'avanu bandanante' hAge nEraDA gi hELadiruvudu (reported speech) sUcisuttade.

#### 9.3 tAnu padada baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sec-9-3)

tAnu reflexive/logophoric pada. nAnu mattu nInu padagaLu yAra Aduge/kELuge enbudu sUcisuttave; tAnu pada yAra bErA sUcisuttade. I pada sollidalli oLagirabbEkAda koNDiyondhige samba ndakkE bandiruttade.

##### 9.3.1 hOlikegaLu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-3-1)

<a id="sub-9-3-2"></a>

##### 9.3.2 pAngu sollinOLagirabbEkemba kaTTale

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-3-2)

tAnu koNDiya (antecedent) A tAnu bandu oLasollinallige sErida mukhya sollinOLage irabbEku — horasollinalli iruvudu sadyakke kaTTale mIrantu. (3k) rAju tAnE bandu — sarI; (3c) *tAnu rAjuvininda bande — tappAguttade.

<a id="sub-9-3-3"></a>

##### 9.3.3 ibbibbarugaLu ADugarU mattu kELugarirUva sollugaLu

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-3-3)

##### 9.3.4 bEre bageyada oLasollugaLalli baLake

[ಕನ್ನಡ →](../kn/full#sub-9-3-4)

##### 9.3.5 sErikeyada sollugaLalli baLake

##### 9.3.6 pattugerUpadalli baLake

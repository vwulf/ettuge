## bhAga 11: arimeya padagaLige kannaDavE mElu
[ಕನ್nnaDa →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-blog#inukunota-11) | [English →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-en#inukunota-11)

> **dinAnka:** 2014-04-14  
> **mUla URL:** <http://dnshankarabhat.net/%e0%b2%85%e0%b2%b0%e0%b2%bf%e0%b2%ae%e0%b3%86%e0%b2%af-%e0%b2%aa%e0%b2%a6%e0%b2%97%e0%b2%b3%e0%b2%bf%e0%b2%97%e0%b3%86-%e0%b2%95%e0%b2%a8%e0%b3%8d%e0%b2%a8%e0%b2%a1%e0%b2%b5%e0%b3%87-%e0%b2%ae/>  

**nuDiyarimeya iNukunOTa – 11**

kannaDadalli **arimeya** (pAribASika) padagaLannu unTumADabEkAdAgalella heccina arivigarU samskruta padagaLannE ArisikoLLuttAre; idakkAgi kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasuvudu tumbA aparUpa.

I kAraNakkAgi, kannaDadavendu hELalAguva heccina arimeya padakOSagaLallU ivattu nUrakke embattaraSTu illavE adakkintalU heccu samskruta padagaLannu kANabahudu. kannaDa arivigara I niluvinindAgi, ivattu kannaDada arimeya barahagaLu samskruta padagaLinda tumbi tuLukuttive.

hindina kAladalli kannaDadalli bareyuvavarige bEkAgiruva heccina tiLivugaLU samskrutadallidduvu; hAgAgi, avannu kannaDakke tarabEkAdAga, adakke bEkAguva arimeya padagaLalli heccinavannU samskrutadindalEnE eravalu paDeyalAguttittu, mattu Avattige adu sariyAda dAriyAgittu.

Adare, ivattu arimeya (vijnAnada) barahagArarige bEkAguva heccina tiLivugaLU ingliS‌nallive; hAgAgi, avannu ingliS‌ninda kannaDakke tarabEkAdAga, adakke bEkAguva padagaLannu ingliS‌ninda eravalu paDeyuvudu, illavE kannaDadallEnE hosadAgi unTumADikoLLuvudu emba eraDu dArigaLu ivattina maTTige sariyAduvu.

hIgiddarU, kannaDada barahagAraru ivattigU arimeya padagaLAgi samskruta padagaLannu baLasuva haLeya sampradAyavannE munduvarisuttiddAre; tamage bEkAgiruva padagaLu samskrutadallillavAdare, hosa padagaLannu samskrutadallEnE unTumADi kannaDakke taruvantaha innondu vicitravAda sampradAyavannU avaru huTTuhAkikonDiddAre.

hIge mADuttiruvudakkAgi, avaru kelavu nevagaLannU koDuttiddAre: ingliS‌nalli arimeya padagaLAgi lyATin illavE grIk padagaLannu baLasuva hAge kannaDadalli samskruta padagaLannu baLasalAguttadeyembudu ivugaLallondu.

Adare, ingliS‌ge lyATin iruva hAge kannaDakke samskruta alla; yAkendare, ingliS mattu lyATin‌gaLu ondE nuDikuTumbakke sErive, mattu kannaDa mattu samskrutagaLu bEre bEre nuDikuTumbagaLige sErive. ingliS‌ge bEkAguva padagaLannu lyATin‌nalli unTumADidAga heccina tondaregaLEnU edurAguvudilla; Adare, kannaDakke bEkAguva padagaLannu samskrutadalli unTumADidAga halavu tondaregaLu edurAguttave:

ingliS‌nalli mukyavAgi navn, vara‍b, mattu ejakTiv embudAgi mUru bageya padagaLive; kannaDadallU intahavE mUru bageya padagaLive. Adare, samskrutadalli mukyavAgi nAmapada mattu kriyApada emba eraDu bageya padagaLu mAtra ive.

hAgAgi, ingliS‌na arimeya padagaLige samanAda padagaLannu samskrutadalli unTumADi kannaDakke tarahOdare, navn mattu ejakTiv‌gaLa naDuvina vyatyAsavannu avugaLalli sariyAgi tOrisalu baruvudilla. I tondareyindAgi, ivattu baLakeyalliruva arimeya padakOSagaLalli tumbA gondalavunTAgiruvudannu kANabahudu.

arimeya barahagaLalli kannaDa padagaLannu baLasidare, avu samskruta padagaLa hAge kacitavAda huruLannu koDalAravu embudu innondu neva; Adare, samskruta padagaLu kannaDadalli unTumADuttiruva mElina gondalavE idannu taLLihAkuttade.

ivattu ingliS‌nalli kampyUTar‌ge sambandisida halavu arimeya padagaLannu dinabaLakeya padagaLannu baLasiyE unTumADalAguttide; mavs ‘ili’, vinDOs ‘kiTiki’, TrI ‘mara’, veb ‘bale’ embantaha I padagaLindAgi gondalavEnU unTAda hAge kANisuvudilla.

hAgAgi, kannaDada arimeya padagaLigAgi samskrutada morehoguva ivattina sampradAyavannu biTTukoDuvudE oLLeyadu. ingliS‌ninda arimeya barahagaLannu kannaDakke taruvAga, kannaDa padagaLa sogaDannu keDisadantaha ingliS padagaLannu uLisikoLLabahudu, mattu adannu keDisuvantaha padagaLa badalu kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasi hosa padagaLannu unTumADikoLLabahudu; idE kannaDakke sariyAda dAri.

illi innondu viSayavannU otti hELabEkAgide: ingliS‌ninda arimeya barahagaLannu kannaDakke taruvAga, eraDu mukya gurigaLigAgi nAvu avugaLalliruva ingliS padagaLige badalAgi kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasalu hOguttEve: hAge mADuva mUlaka baraha Enannu tiLisuttade embudu kannaDigarige sulabavAgi tiLiyuvantAgabEku embudondu, mattu kannaDada sogaDu keDadante uLiyabEku embudinnondu.

ittIcege kampyUTar‌gaLige sambandisida kelavu kaypiDigaLalli heccina ingliS padagaLannU hAgeyE uLisiruvudannu kANabahudu. ingliS padagaLa huruLu gottiruvavaru mAtra avannu Odi tiLidukoLLaballaru, mattu avugaLalli kannaDada sogaDu pUra‍ti keTTuhOgiruttade; endare, mElina eraDu gurigaLannU avu talapuvudE illa.

intaha kaypiDigaLalli baruva ingliS padagaLige badalAgi samskruta padagaLannu baLasidarU pariNAma ondE: avannu Odi tiLiyalu ingliS padagaLa badalu samskruta padagaLa huruLu gottirabEku, mattu avu kannaDada sogaDannu keDisadiruvudilla. endare, intaha barahagaLU mElina eraDu gurigaLannu talapuvudE illa.

halavu heccina samskruta padagaLa huruLugaLannu kaliyuvudakkintalU mUla barahadallidda ingliS padagaLa huruLugaLannu kaliyuvudE kannaDigara maTTige oLLeyadallavE? yAkendare, munde heccina tiLivannu paDeyabEkendiruvavarige ingliS padagaLa tiLivu neravAgaballudu; Adare, samskruta padagaLa tiLivu yAva neravannU nIDalAravu.

arimeya barahagaLinda tiLivannu paDeyuvudu mAtravallade, adannu orege haccuva mattu avugaLallillada hosa tiLivannu kanDuhiDiyuva kelasavannU nAvindu naDesabEkAgide. heccu heccu samskruta illavE ingliS padagaLannu baLasiruva barahagaLannu kaSTapaTTu ariyuvudaralliyE namma samayavella kaLeduhOguttade. avannu orege haccuva mattu hosa tiLivannu kanDuhiDiyuva kelasadallU namma kalivigaru toDagabEkiddalli, arimeya padagaLalli heccinavU kannaDadavE AgirabEku.

## bhAga 2: maneya horagaDe aDigemane pAkaSAleyAgabEkE?
[ಕನ್nnaDa →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-blog#inukunota-2) | [English →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-en#inukunota-2)

> **dinAnka:** 2014-04-14  
> **mUla URL:** <https://dnshankarabhat.net/%e0%b2%ae%e0%b2%a8%e0%b3%86%e0%b2%af-%e0%b2%b9%e0%b3%8a%e0%b2%b0%e0%b2%97%e0%b2%a1%e0%b3%86-%e0%b2%85%e0%b2%a1%e0%b2%bf%e0%b2%97%e0%b3%86%e0%b2%ae%e0%b2%a8%e0%b3%86-%e0%b2%aa%e0%b2%be%e0%b2%95/>  

**nuDiyarimeya iNukunOTa – 2**

kannaDigara **padabaLakeya** viSayadalli ondu sOjigada sangatiyide. adEnendu tiLiyalu kelavu ettugegaLannu tegedukoLLONa:

maneyalli aDigemane, UTadakONe ennuva nAvu sammELanagaLantaha sandara‍bagaLalli avugaLa badalAgi pAkaSAle, bOjanaSAle ennuttEve. baTTeyangaDige hOguttEne ennuttEve; Adare, antaha angaDigaLige hesaru koDabEkAdAga, vastrAlaya illavE vastrabanDAra emba hesarugaLaSTE hoLeyuttave. tinDigaLannu mAruva angaDi tinDimaneyAgibiTTare adEnO mujugara, upahAragruhavE AgabEku.

nAyiya kuritu bareda barahadalli uddakkU nAyi emba padavE baLakeyAgideyAdarU, talebarahadalli mAtra SvAnaviddaraSTE nemmadi. maduveya kareyOle kaLuhisuva badalu vivAha AmantraNa kaLisidare mAtra maduvege baruttArEnO emba anisike nammalli halavaralliddantide.

hAgeyE, pustakagaLige hesaru koDuvallU baLakeyalliruva padagaLiginta baLakeyallillada mattu heccu mandige tiLiyada padagaLE candavendu halavu barahagArarige anisuttade. manegaLige, angaDigaLige, kUTagaLige, illavE halavu bageya hammugegaLige hesaru koDabEkAgiruvallU namma oDalininda nEravAgi baruva padagaLu takkavendu tOruvudE illa; dUradara‍Sana, dinapatrike, uccaSikSaNa, nirudyOga samasye, janagaNane, lOkAyukta, paTyapustaka modalAda halavu bageya padagaLannu unTumADuvalli kUDa idE sOjigavannu kANabahudu.

intaha halavAru ‘mElmaTTada’ sannivESagaLalli namma nAligegaLige tantAnE baruva padagaLu – endare kannaDadavE Ada padagaLu – baLakege takkavalla, mEle kANisidante samskrutadinda eravalu paDeda padagaLE takkavu emba kILarime nammalli heccinavarallide. idondu sOjigavallade mattEnu?

hindina kAladalli kannaDa nuDi **samskrutada** kaLegeTTa rUpa emba anisike halavu kannaDa panDitarallittu; kannaDa padagaLiginta samskruta padagaLu mElmaTTadavu embudAgi avaru tiLiyuttiddudakke I tappu anisike kAraNavAgittu; Adare, nijakkU kannaDa samskrutada kaLegeTTa rUpa alla, adakkinta tIra bErAgiruva ondu svatantravAda nuDi embudannu ivattu nAvu kanDukonDiddEve. hAgAgi, ivattigU samskruta padagaLu kannaDa padagaLinda mElmaTTadavu endu bAvisuvudakke yAva AdAravU illa.

I rIti hosapadagaLannu unTumADabEkAgiruvalli kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasadiruvudakke avugaLa kuritAgi kILarime iruvudu ondu kAraNavAdare, tamage bEkAgiruva hosa padagaLannu unTumADuva aLavu kannaDakkilla emba tappu kalpane innondu kAraNa. samskrutadalliruvaSTu oTTu(pratyaya)gaLu mattu padagaLu kannaDadallilla; hAgAgi, halavu bageya padagaLannu hosadAgi unTumADuvalli samskrutada morehogade bEre dAriyE illa emba anisike halavarallide.

Adare, idu tappu anisike. entaha viSayavannu bEkiddarU tiLisalu bEkAguvantaha padagaLannu mattu sollugaLannu unTumADuva aLavu kannaDakkide. ellA svAbAvikavAgi beLedubanda nuDigaLigU intaha aLavu iruttade. kannaDada I aLavu entahadu embudannu sariyAgi tiLidukonDu, adannu baLasi, kannaDakke bEkAgiruva hosa padagaLannu kannaDadallEnE unTumADalu baruttade. antaha hosa padagaLiruva baravaNigeyannu heccu heccu kannaDigaru sulabavAgi Odi tiLidukoLLaballaru, mattu baravaNigeya mUlaka naDeyuva carcegaLalli sakriyavAgi pAlgoLLalU ballaru. baravaNigeya guriyE idallave?

ellA nuDigaLigU avugaLadE Ada padagaLa sogaDu embudiruttade; kannaDa padagaLa sogaDu samskruta padagaLa sogaDiginta tIra bErAgide. kannaDa barahadalli heccu heccu samskruta padagaLannu baLasidantella, adara padagaLa sogaDu mareyAguttA hOguttade, mattu adannu Odi tiLiyuva kelasa kannaDigarige heccu heccu toDakinadAguttA hOguttade.

hAgAgi, kannaDa barahagaLalli heccu heccu eravalu padagaLannu baLasahOgade, adara padagaLa sogaDannu uLisikoLLabEku ennuvudu bariya abimAnada praSne mAtra alla; kannaDa baraha ellA kannaDigarannU talapabEku mattu ellA kannaDigarindalU horabarabEkemba mukyavAda guriyannu muTTuva praSneyU havdu.

ittIcege kannaDadalli arimeya (pAribASika) padagaLannu unTumADuvavaru I viSayavannu pUra‍ti maretiruvante kANuttade. kannaDadavE Ada padagaLu avara kaNNige kANisade hOguttive. avaru unTumADiruva padakOSagaLalli nUrakke tombattaraSTu samskrutadinda eravalu paDeda, illavE samskrutadalli hosadAgi unTumADi kannaDakke eravalu tanda padagaLE kANisikoLLuttave; intaha padakOSagaLannu kannaDa padakOSagaLendu kareyuttiruvudu nijakkU accariya viSayavAgide!

ingliS‌nalliruva halavu arimeya padagaLu kannaDada sollugaLalli sariyAgi hondikoLLuvudilla, avannu baLasida sollugaLu sikkalu sikkalAgi kANisikoLLuttave, mattu avannu Odi tiLidukoLLuva kelasa kannaDigarige tumbA toDakinadAguttade. hAgAgi, nAvu avugaLa badalu bEre kannaDadavE Ada arimeya padagaLannu unTumADalu hOguttEve.

Adare, hAge padagaLannu unTumADabEkiruvalli kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasuva badalu samskruta padagaLannu baLasidevAdare, nammavE Ada padagaLannu unTumADuvudara hindiruva mukyavAda guriyannu nAvu talapuvudE illa. yAkendare, ingliS eravalugaLa hAge halavu samskruta eravalugaLU kannaDada sollugaLalli sariyAgi hondikoLLuvudilla, mattu avannu baLasida sollugaLu sikkalu sikkalAgiyE uLiyuttave. avannu Odi, avu tiLisuva viSayagaLannu tiLidukoLLuva kelasavU tumbA toDakinadAgiyE uLiyuttade.

samskruta padagaLannu baLasi paLagiruva kannaDa barahagArarige mattu Odugarige tumbA samskruta padagaLannu baLasiruva barahagaLU aSTondu toDakinavendu anisadirabahudu. idakke kAraNavEnendare, avaru kannaDa padagaLa sogaDannu mAtravallade samskruta padagaLa sogaDannU tammadAgisikonDiruttAre.

Adare, heccina kannaDigarigU antaha barahagaLu toDakinavAgi kANisuttave; avara ADunuDigaLalli tumbA kaDime samskruta eravalugaLu baLakeyAguttiruvudu, mattu hAge baLakeyAguttiruvavU kannaDa padagaLa sogaDige hondikeyAguvante mAra‍paTTiruvudu idakke kAraNa.

hAgAgi, kannaDa barahagaLu ellA kannaDigarannU talapabEkiddalli, mattu ellA kannaDigarindalU horahommabEkiddalli, avugaLalli baLakeyAguva eravalu padagaLannu, avu samskrutadavirali illavE ingliSinavirali, AdaSTu kaDime mADalu prayatnisabEku, mattu baLasalEbEkendiruvavu kannaDada sogaDannu keDisadante avugaLalli mAra‍pADugaLannu mADikoLLabEku.

ellA maTTada baLakegaLigU kannaDa padagaLu takkudAduvu embudannu kannaDada barahagAraru mattu kannaDadalli hosa padagaLannu unTumADuvavaru managANabEku, mattu mElmaTTada baLakegaLige kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu baLasuvudu hemmeya viSayavaSTE alla, baravaNigeya mukya guriyannu muTTuva bageyU havdemba anisike avaralli beLedubarabEku.

## bhAga 3: padagaLannu Oduva hAgeyE bareyabEku

**nuDiyarimeya iNukunOTa – 3**

kannaDadalli kannaDadavE Ada padagaLannu Oduva hAgeyE bareyuttEve; Adare, samskrutadinda eravalu paDeda padagaLannu mAtra Oduva hAge bareyade, heccukaDime samskrutadalliruva hAgeyE bareyuttEve: viSESa embudAgi OdidarU viSESa embudAgi bareyuttEve, mattu adikAri embudAgi OdidarU adhikAri embudAgi bareyuttEve.

ondarinda enTara varegina eNikepadagaLu, mattu hattu, nUru emba padagaLu otte padagaLu; hannondarinda hattombattara varegina padagaLalli hattu padada pattugerUpakke ondarinda ombattaravaregina padagaLannu sErisalAgide. adakke mEle, ippattu embudaralli eraDu (ip) padakke hattu (pattu) padavannu nEravAgi sErisalAgide. idE rItiyalli, mundeyU ippattarinda ippattombattara varegina padagaLalli ippattu padada pattugerUpakke ondarinda ombattara varegina padagaLannu sErisalAgide (ippattondu, ippatteraDu), mattu mUvattu padadalli mUru padakke hattu (vattu) padavannu nEravAgi sErisalAgide.

munde tombattombattara varegU idE rItiyalli I eraDu holabugaLa baLakeyAgide: modala ombattu eNikepadagaLannu pattugerUpada mUlaka unTumADalAgide, mattu hattaneya padavannu nAlku, aydu, Aru modalAda padagaLige nEravAgi hattu padavannu sErisi unTumADalAgide. nUru embudannu tiLisalu mAtra otte padada baLakeyAgide.

Adare, **samskruta**dalli mattu adarinda beLedu bandiruva**hindi**, **marATi** modalAda inDO-Ara‍yan nuDigaLalli eNikeya padagaLannu bEreyE bageyalli kaTTalAgiddu, avakkU ankegaLigU naDuve intaha hondANike kANisuvudilla. ettugegAgi, hindiyalli ‘aDtIs’ emba eNikepadadalli ‘8-3(hattu)’ emba OraNavide; I OraNa 38 emba ankeyalliruva OraNakkinta bErAgide; samskrutadallU eppattaydu emba huruLannu koDuva ‘pancasaptati’ emba eNikepadadalli 5-7(hattu) emba OraNaviddu, adu 75 emba ankeyalliruva OraNakkinta bErAgide.

I holabinante, ippattAru ennalu ‘ondippattu ondnAlku eraDu’ ennabEku, mattu eppattamUru ennalu ‘mUrippattu mUrnAlku ondu’ (3 x 20) + (3 x 4) + 1 ennabEku. intaha eNike nammadarinda toDakAdudendu namage tOrabahudu, Adare bODo nuDiyalli mAtanADuvavarige nammalliruva hattara holabiginta avara nAlkara holabE oLLeyadendu anisadiradu.

nammalli eNikeya padagaLannu unTumADuvalli hattara holabu baLakeyallideyAda kAraNa, adE hattara holabinalli unTumADiruva lITar, rupAyi, grAm modalAda aLatepadagaLa baLake modaligidda rAtalu, sEru, aDi modalAdavugaLiginta oLLeyavendu anisuttade; Adare, nAlkara holabannu baLasuvavarige avu hindinavakkinta oLLeyavendEnU anisabEkAgilla.

eNikepadagaLa baLakeyallU nuDigaLa naDuve halavu bageya vyatyAsagaLannu kANabahudu: kannaDadalli eNikepadagaLannu mara, mane, huDuga, cenDu modalAda padagaLige nEravAgi sErisalu baruttade (mUru manegaLu, ippattondu cenDugaLu); Adare, nIru, akki, eNNe, hAlu modalAda padagaLige eNikepadagaLannu sErisabEkiddalli, modalige avakke aLateyannu tiLisuva padagaLannu sErisabEkAguttade (mUru kilO akki, eraDu lITar hAlu). idakke badalu, bODo nuDiyalli yAva padakkU eNikepadagaLannu nEravAgi sErisalu baruvudilla; ondu pada tiLisuva vyakti illavE vastu entahadu embudannu aDakavAgi tiLisuva padavannu adakke modalu sErisi, AmEle eNike padagaLannu sErisabEkAguttade.

intaha bEreyU halavu vyatyAsagaLu nuDigaLalli baLakeyAguva eNikeya holabugaLa naDuve kANisikoLLuttave. ivellavU eNikeyannu baLasuvalli mAnavanu kanDukonDiruva jANmeya holabugaLendu hELabahudu.

## bhAga 29: padagaLa hinnaDavaLiyannu ariyuva bage
[ಕನ್nnaDa →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-blog#inukunota-29) | [English →](./18-kannaDa-nuDiya-bagege-cintane-en#inukunota-29)

**nuDiyarimeya iNukunOTa – 29**

kannaDada yAva padavannu bEkiddarU samskrutada padagaLinda banduvembudAgi tOrisikoDaballevu emba nambike kelavarallide; hIge tOrisikoDuvudakkAgi avaru padagaLa uligaLalli mattu huruLugaLalli kANisuva hOlikegaLannu mAtravallade halavAru bageya kaycaLakagaLannU baLasuttAre.

I kaycaLakagaLa mUlaka avaru uli illavE huruLugaLalli hOlikegaLu kANisadiddarU sambandavannu kANaballaru; ettugegAgi, kannaDada ‘nettaru’ padavannu samskrutada ‘rudhira’ padadinda paDeyaballaru; illi huruLina hOlikeyiddarU uliya hOlike tumbA kaDime; kannaDada ‘kaTTe’ padavannu samskrutada ‘kakSA’ (kankuLu) padadinda paDeyaballaru; illi huruLina hOlikeyU tumbA kaDime. hIgiddarU, kaycaLakagaLa mUlaka avaru ivannu ondakkondu jODisi biDuttAre. intaha kaycaLakagaLu avarige tamma nambikeyannu uLisikoLLuvalli neravAguttaveyAdarU, nijakkU A padagaLa hinnaDavaLi (caritre) entahadu embudannu tiLidukoLLuvalli avu yAva neravannU nIDuvudilla.

kannaDada padagaLu eraDu bagegaLalli samskruta padagaLondige sambandisiraballuvu: averaDU ondE nuDikuTumbada nuDigaLembudondu bage, mattu avugaLalli ondu innondarinda padagaLannu eravalu paDedideyembudinnondu bage. hAgAgi, yAva bageya sambandavannu avugaLa padagaLa naDuve kANalAguttade embudannu modalu tiLisabEkAguttade.

nuDigaLalli kAladinda kAlakke halavu bageya mAr‍pADugaLu naDeyuttalE iruttave; ivu uligaLannu mAr‍paDisabahudu, padagaLannu illavE avugaLa huruLugaLannu mAr‍paDisabahudu, mattu avugaLa baLakeya hindiruva kaTTalegaLannu mAr‍paDisabahudu. intaha halavu bageya mAr‍pADugaLu naDedudarindAgi haLegannaDaviddudu naDugannaDavAgi ivattu hosagannaDavAgide, mattu hosagannaDavU halavu bageya ADunuDigaLAgi oDedukonDide.

kAladinda kAlakke naDeyuva intaha mAr‍pADugaLalli nuDiya uligaLalli naDeyuva mAr‍pADugaLE tumbA mukyavAdavugaLu; yAkendare, avugaLindAgi padagaLa mattu oTTu(pratyaya)gaLa rUpadalli mAtravallade, avugaLa baLakeya hindiruva kaTTalegaLallU mAr‍pADugaLu naDeyuttave. kannaDada hinnaDavaLiyalli intaha halavu bageya uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLu naDedive.

haLegannaDada pakAra hakAravAgiruvudu (pAlu>hAlu, paLe>haLe, pOgu>hOgu); ra-Ra, La-ZagaLa naDuvina vyatyAsa illavAgiruvudu, udda tereyuli (svara) mattu taDeyuli(spara‍Sa)gaLa naDuvina mUguli (anunAsika) bidduhOgiruvudu (nUnku>nUku, dAnTu>dATu) modalAduvu intaha uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLu.

Adare beLLage, beccage, neTTage embantaha padagaLalli naDedilla). idallade, intaha mAr‍pADugaLigella ondu gaDuviruttade; I gaDuvu mugida mEle hindina barahagaLinda (pAlu, puTTa, peTTu) illavE bEre nuDigaLinda (pApa, puNya, pUje) padagaLu eravalAgi bandu I mAr‍pADige horapaDikegaLAgi kANisaballuvu.

eraDu nuDigaLu ondE nuDikuTumbakke sEriveyendu tIra‍mAnisuvallella intaha uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLannE mukyavAda neleyAgi baLasikoLLalAguttade. yAkendare, nuDigaLalli naDeyuva halavu bageya mAr‍pADugaLalli uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLa mEle mAtra padagaLa huruLugaLu yAva prabAvavannU bIruvudilla; nEravAgi padagaLalli illavE avugaLa baLakeya hindiruva kaTTalegaLalli naDeyuva mAr‍pADugaLa mEle avu halavu bagegaLalli prabAvavannu bIruttave, mattu I rIti huruLugaLinda prabAvitavAgiruva mAr‍pADugaLannu kacitavAda kaTTalegaLalli serehiDiyalu baruvudilla; yAkendare, huruLugaLinda prabAvitavAda mAr‍pADugaLige halavAru bageya horapaDikegaLiruttave.

hAgAgi, kannaDa mattu samskruta nuDigaLu ondE nuDikuTumbakke sEriveyemba tIr‍mAnakke barabEkiddalli kannaDada hinnaDavaLiyalli naDeda uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLannE mukya neleyAgi baLasikoLLabEkAguttade. endare, kannaDa mattu samskruta padagaLa naDuve kANisuva vyatyAsagaLu kannaDada hinnaDavaLiyalli naDedidda halavu bageya uligaLa mAr‍pADugaLindAgi mUDibandive embudAgi tOrisikoDalu sAdyavAdAga mAtra kannaDa samskrutadinda beLedubandide embudannu oppikoLLalu baruttade. Adare, iduvarege yArigU A rIti tOrisikoDalu sAdyavAgilla.

hAgAgi, sadyakkantU kannaDa mattu samskruta nuDigaLu ondE nuDikuTumbakke sErillavembudannu oppikoLLabEkAguttade, mattu kannaDadalli mUladrAviDa nuDiyinda banda kannaDadavE Ada padagaLu, mattu samskrutavE modalAda bEre nuDigaLinda eravalAgi banda padagaLu embudAgi eraDu bageya padagaLu iveyembudannU oppikoLLabEkAguttade.

kannaDada padagaLigU samskrutada padagaLigU naDuve avugaLa uligaLalli mattu huruLugaLalli hOlikeyideyAdare, adakke halavu kAraNagaLiraballuvu. ettugegAgi, I ellA padagaLannU unTumADidavaru mAnavarE embudondu kAraNa; hOlike bariya AkasmikavAgirabahudembudinnondu kAraNa. hAgAgi, ondu nuDi innondarinda eravalu paDedideyembudu padagaLa naDuviruva hOlikegaLigiruva halavu kAraNagaLalli ondu mAtra. idallade, illiyU ondu padavannu yAva nuDi yAva nuDiyinda eravalAgi paDedide embudannu tIr‍mAnisuvalli arakege neleyide.

sAmAnyavAgi, ondu padada harahu entahadu embudara mEle adara hinnaDavaLiyentahadu embudannu tIr‍mAnisalAguttade; ettugegAgi, kannaDada ondu padakke samAnavAda padagaLu tamiLu, telugu, gOnDi, kuDuk modalAda bEre halavu drAviDa nuDigaLallU kANisikoLLuttiveyAdare, mattu samskrutavannu horatupaDisi uLida lyATin, grIk, par‍Siyan modalAda inDO-yurOpiyan nuDigaLalli kANisuvudillavAdare, adu kannaDaddE Ada pada, mattu samskrutakkEnE adu drAviDa nuDigaLinda eravalAgi bandide endu hELabEkAguttade.

intaha arakegaLannu naDesade bariya hOlikeya mEle ellavU samskrutadinda bandiveyendu hELuvudannu ondu arimeya hELikeyendu tiLiyalu baruvudilla.

## bhAga 33: kannaDa nuDiya sogaDu

**nuDiyarimeya iNukunOTa – 33**

ellA **nuDi**gaLigU avugaLadE Ada ondu **sogaDu**embudiruttade. idannu barahagaLu heccu keDadante uLisikoLLabEku. illavAdare avu tamma jIvantikeyannu kaLedukoLLuttave, mattu sAmAnya janarinda dUravAguttave. antaha barahagaLannu kaliyuva mattu baLasuva kelasavU A nuDiyannADuva janarige tumbA toDakinadAguttade. nuDigaLigiruva I sogaDu avugaLa padagaLalli baruva uligaLalli, uligaLa OraNagaLalli, padagaLige oTTu(pratyaya)gaLannu illavE bEre padagaLannu sErisidAga avugaLa uligaLalli naDeyuva mArpADugaLalli, padagaLannu sErisi bEre padagaLannu unTumADuva bagegaLalli, mattu padagaLinda padakantegaLannu mattu sollugaLannu unTumADuva bagegaLalli kANisikoLLuttade.

kannaDakkU intaha tannadE Ada ondu sogaDide, mattu adu samskruta, hindi, ingliS modalAda bEre nuDigaLigiruva sogaDiginta tIra bErAgide; kannaDada uligaLu, avugaLa OraNagaLu, avugaLa sErikeya mArpADugaLu, kannaDadalli pada, padakante mattu sollugaLannu unTumADuva bagegaLu modalAduvannu samskrutadantaha bEre nuDigaLalli kANisuva bagegaLondige hOlisi nODidAga, nijakkU kannaDada sogaDu entahadu embudu gottAguttade.

(1) samskruta padagaLalli baLakeyAguva khachaThathapha mattu ghajhaDhadhabhagaLemba ottusirina barigegaLu (mahAprANAkSaragaLu) kannaDa padagaLallilla; Adare, samskruta padagaLalli kANisadiruva giDDa ekAra mattu okAragaLu kannaDa padagaLallive. samskrutadalliruva xkAra mattu SakAragaLu kannaDadallilla; Adare, samskrutadallillada ey, oy, uy, Ay, ov, iv modalAda halavu svara mattu yakAra illavE vakAragaLa jODigaLu kannaDadallive.

(2) I eraDu nuDigaLigU samAnavAgiruva akSaragaLu halaviveyAdarU avu ondE rItiyAgi baLakeyAguvudilla. samskrutadalli ottakSaragaLinda modalAguva padagaLu sAkaSTive (krAnti, kSame, jYAna, jyOti, dvESa, mlEccha). Adare kannaDadavE Ada padagaLalli A rIti ottakSaragaLu padagaLa modalige baruvudilla. padagaLa naDuve baraballa ottakSaragaLallU samskruta mattu kannaDa nuDigaLa naDuve vyatyAsavide. samskrutada bhakta, ajYAna, vatsa, matsya modalAda padagaLalli naDuve bandiruva kt, jY, ts, tsy modalAda halavu bageya ottakSaragaLu kannaDa padagaLalli kANisuvudilla.

(3) eraDu illavE heccu padagaLannu oTTusErisi hosa padagaLannu tayArisuva holabugaLu I eraDu nuDigaLalli bEre bErAgive. samskrutadalli sAmAnyavAgi hesarupadagaLannu mAtra intaha racanegaLalli baLasalAguttade; Adare, kannaDadalli hesarupada (talegUdalu, bayalATa), esakapada (cuccumaddu, kaDegOlu) mattu paricepadagaLemba (doDDamma, bisinIru) mUru bageya padagaLannU intaha racanegaLalli baLasalu baruttade.

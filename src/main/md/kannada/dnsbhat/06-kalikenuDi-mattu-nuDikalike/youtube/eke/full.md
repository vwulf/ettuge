## viShaya 1 — tAynuDi: kalikege adE ADi

*bhAgagaLu P1, P2, 1–3*

*(bhAgagaLu P1, 1, 3 kannaDavagalilla)*

DNS baT avara pustaka varNaneyinda:

makkaLu tamma modalane nuDiyanu yAru kalisade svAbAvikavAgi kalikoLLuttAre. ee nuDiyane ADikiliyAgi baLasi, eraDane nuDiyanu kaLiyuvudu ellarige sAdhya. AdakarE ee ADikali tOLisiddare — kannaDa makkaLige ingliSinallE ella viShayagaLannu kalisidare — hattu makkaLalli heccina makkaLu sariyanE kaLiyalAraru.

kalikenuDi embudu makkaLu yaava nuDiyalli viShayagaLanu kaLiyuttAre — I artha. nuDikalike embudu nuDiyanne ondu viShayavAgi kaLiyuvudu — I artha. I eraDu padagaLu onDAnnonDige tilubaDiside: modalane nuDinallE kalikeyAgabEku, nantara nuDiyanu kaLiyabahUdu.

## viShaya 2 — nuDikalike: mUDE mEle mUDE bEDa

*bhAgagaLu 4–8*

*(bhAgagaLu 5, 6, 7 kannaDavagalilla; bhAga 8 — ondu vAkya mAtra)*

bhAga 4 — keyword saMkEtagaLu dorakuttave: vyAkaraNa, vibhakti pratyayagaLu, telugu, tamiLu, japAn, cIna, koriyA, pOrcugal. ee dESagaLa udAharaNagaLu nuDi-kalika nItiya hOrATadalli bEre bhAShe-viShaya makkaLige hEge marudukoLLuttave endu tOruttave.

## viShaya 3 — ingliS mAdhyama: tondare mattu pariNAma

*bhAgagaLu 13–17*

*(bhAgagaLu 9, 10, 11, 12 kannaDavagalilla)*

bhAga 13 — keyword saMkEtagaLu: abhivxddhi, internet, kAlEju, hAstel, saMSOdhane, ingliS. ee padagaLu ingliS mAdhyama SikSaNada mEle saMSOdhana viSlEShaNe naDeyuttiruva saMdarbhakke hELuttave.

bhAga 14 — ondu aMSa: "hOlikE mADuvAga bahaLaSTu teranADinalli… samskxtadalli paddhatigaLannu nODidE AdakarE illi patte mADalAgalilla anta" — hOlike mADaladE, samskxta modalanu nODi illi upayOgilla anta tIrmanisida aMSa. artha-aMSa dorakide adarE paThya saMpUrNa lagalilla.

bhAga 17 — mAtra eraDu padagaLu: "ingliS mAdhyama ingliS mAdhyama" — I viShayave pustakada mukhya AgrahavAgide endu saMkEtisuttade.

DNS baT avara ustaka varNaneyinda: hosa mAdhyama bhASheyannu (ingliSannu) kalikeyalli baLasikoLLalu aindu-ARu varSha bEkAguttade. A hindinalli kalisidare, baDavaru hecca kaSTa paduttAre; mattu kali bagge siddhiyilla.

## viShaya 4 — ellara kannaDa: mane nuDiyinda SAlE nuDige

*bhAgagaLu 18–19*

*(bhAgagaLu 18, 19 kannaDavagalilla)*

DNS baT avara pustaka varNaneyinda: SAlE, gaNita, viJJAna, ella viShayagaLannu makkaLa tAynuDinallE kaLisabEku. nantara, SAlEgaLu makkaLige "ellara kannaDa" — sadharaNavAda sAmAjika kannaDa — hEge mAtanADuvudu mattu bareyuvudu endu kalisabahUdu. ee badalAvaNe tAynuDigintoLagE naDeyabEku — hOragin nuDi modalallE tappisiddare, kalike neLagaTTuvudilla.

## viShaya 1 — eraDu vidAnagaLu: likhita caritre mattu kalpita caritre

*bhAga 1*

[📼 bhAga 1 →](../kn/full#part-1)

kannaDa bhASheya cariterannu tiLiyabEkAdarE eraDu vidAnagaLu iruttave: likhita caritre mattu kalpita caritre. eraDU AvaSyaka; ondondu vidAna onDottondannu pogaListE hOguttade.

**likhita caritre:** SAsan, kAvya, gadya ivugaLannu ShatabmAnada mULaka beLayisuttA hEge kannaDa badalAyitu enbanudu kaNDupiDisuvudu. sumAru 1,500 varSagaLa hindina kannaDa SAsan ivugaLu kachcha sAmagri.

**kalpita caritre (§munna):** tamiLu, telugu, gonDi, kuDux, maLayALa, kui emba drAviDa bhAShe mattu kannaDa oLanuDigaLannu tulanisuttA mUla draviDa bhAShe (mUla drAviDa) mattu mUla kannaDa ivugaLannu maru-nirmANa mADuvudu. A A mUla bhAShe yavanU nEra daxle baralavillE — kalpita nuDi. adarE I vidAnada mULaka SAsan daxlEyO hiMdina kAladalli kUDa kannaDa iTTaLannu tiLiyabahudu.

> *(eraDU vidAnagaLU onDottondannu purIkarisikoLLuttave — ondu vidAnada kamigaLannu innOndu vidAna tumbuttade)*

## viShaya 2 — likhita cariteya miti mattu toDakugaLu

*bhAga 1*

[📼 bhAga 1 →](../kn/full#part-1)

likhita caritre heccu sAkShiyAgiddarU halavAru toDakugaLu iruttave:

**prAdEsika mattu jAti bhEda:** onDE ShatabmAnadalli beleda eraDu SAsan prAdEsika bhEda tOruttave (dharavADa mattu maisUru), illave jAti bhEda (vokkaligu, brahmiN, linga yat). A bhEda kAlada badalAvaNeyO, prAdEsika bhEdavO endu taralUDEgAgu.

**baraha mAtu hindE:** bhASheya badalAvaNe mATinalli saMbhavaguttade; baraha A badalAvaNeyanu taDavAgiyE grahisuttade. hAgAgi, ondu paThyada kAla A paThyadalli kANuva bhAShA rUpagaLu AguvaLLige kaLeditu endu hELuvudu sarialla.

**kRitaka prAcIna baraha:** buDDaNNa ADhu kannaDigarAgiddA haLegannaDadalli kavana Adaru — A kavanAnu kAlasUciyAgi baLasuvaLLi caturateyiMda tapputtEve.

**lipiya miti:** kannaDa lipi bhASheya sarva svaravyanjana bhEdagaLannu tOruvudilla; haTTu haLe lipigaLu I kamigaLannu heccAgiyE anubhavisuttave. hAgAgi SAsan daxlegaLinda saMpUrNa dhvani mAhiti dorakuvudilla.

## viShaya 3 — tulanAtmaka vidAna: k/c antaravina udAharaNe

*bhAga 2*

[📼 bhAga 2 →](../kn/full#part-2)

**tulanAtmaka vidAna (§1):** eraDu sambandha bhAShe geLeyuvudu; ellA niyamita bhEda geLeyuvudu; prateya bhEdannu eraDu bhAShe bErEyAgida nantara naDeda svaramArpADininda vivarisikoLLuvudu. I rItiyalli nirmANa mADida pUrva bhAShe 'kalpita nuDi' — yAranU nEra baredilla, adarE niyamita tarkadinda tIrmAnisabahudu.

**kannaDa–tamiLu k/c svaramArpADu:**

| kannaDa | tamiLu | artha |
|---------|--------|-------|
| ketta | cetta | keDagu |
| kEri | cEri | bIdi |
| kEne | cEne | cEne (tarkAri) |
| kelavu | cila | kevalu |
| kere | cirai | kere |
| kilubu | cilumpu | TUSU |

prateya jODiyalli kannaDa modale 'k' iruttade; tamiLu modale 'c' iruttade — matte A 'k'/'c' munne munde oLa-svaravugaLu (i, e, E) iruttave. oLa-svaravugaLu modale iruvA 'k'annu munde sarisuttave; I rIti k→c svaramArpADu tamiLudalli naDeyitu.

**badalAvaNe yAva diSeyalli?** kannaDada mUla drAviDa 'k' uLidukoNDide; tamiLu A 'k'annu 'c'ge badalAyisitu. sAkSi: tamiLudalli modale oLa-svaravugaLu bandu 'k' iruva udAharaNe illE; kannaDadalli eraDU rUpagaLU iruttave (k+oLa, c+oLa).

## viShaya 4 — svabhAvika vidAna mattu svaravina badalAvaNe

*bhAga 2*

[📼 bhAga 2 →](../kn/full#part-2)

**Antarika vidAna:** ondu bhASheya oLagE naDeyuvudu — bEre bhASheyonDige tulaneyillada. I vidAna miti kuDi iruttade; adarE saMbandha bhAShe dorakalilla eMdAga upayogavAguttade.

**nanjanagUDu–puTTUru svaravina badalAvaNe:**

| nanjanagUDu (vokkaliga) | puTTUru (havyaka) | artha |
|-------------------------|-------------------|-------|
| iLi | eLi | iLi |
| siDi | seDi | hOgaLuvudu |
| kudi | kodi | uttu |
| kuNi | koNi | ADuvudu |
| tuLi | toLi | taLLuvudu |

**badalAvaNe yAva diSeyalli?** havyaka rUpagaLu (e/o svaravuLLavugaLu) mUla kannaDa — nanjanagUDu rUpagaLu (i/u svaravuLLavugaLu) navaIna. I svaramArpADu e/o → i/u — nantarEya oLa-svaravina prabhAvadinda prathama svara E lada vargakke hOyitu.

**sAkShi — pUrva haLegannaDa (5–6ne ShatabmAna):**

| pUrva haLegannaDa | haLegannaDa | artha |
|-------------------|-------------|-------|
| kesu | kisu | kOti |
| periya | piriya / hiriya | hiriya |
| edir | idir / idiru | imda |
| pogu | pugu | hOguvudu |
| poruL | puruL | artha / vastu |

pUrva haLegannaDa e/o rUpagaLu havyaka rUpagaLannu purIkarisi, tulanAtmaka vidAnada nirmANadalli mUla kannaDa e/o enbanudu khAtriyAguttade. I rIti baraha daxle mattu tulanAtmaka daxle onDottondannu purIkarisikoLLuttave.

## viShaya 1 — mahAprANa yAke bEDa: mUla vaada

*bhAgagaLu P1, P2, 1–3*

[📼 bhAgagaLu P1–3 →](../kn/full#part-p1)

> *(bhAgagaLu P1, P2, 1, 2, 3 kannaDavagalilla — mUla paThya dorakalavillDa)*

kannaDa mAtinalli mahAprANa uccAraNe illa. "bhArata" ennuvalli kannaDigaraddu "bArata" enba uccAraNe. "dharma" ennuvalli "darma". I uccAraNe bedala sariyAgi sAbitisuttade: kannaDada svantada uccAraNe vyavastheyalli aspirated mattu unaspirated — eraDu bErE bErE uli (phoneme) alla; ondE uli.

kannaDigarE bArI mahAprANa akSaragaLannu kaLitu bereyuvudu sumAru 1500–2000 varSagaLa hindE naDeyitu. kAraNa: kannaDa vidvAnsaru samskxtada mElariMe hoDitu A lipi vyavastEyanu ayke mADigiddarindu. I ayke bhASha viJJAnada neLe mElE tokkaTavalla.

mahAprANa akSaragaLu — Ka, Ga, Ca, Ja, Tha, Dha, tha, dha, pha, bha — ivugaLu kannaDa svantada padagaLige avaSyavE illa. samskxta eravalu padagaLannu bareyalU nIDiva mahAprANa — kannaDigaru hEge uccAraNe mADuttArO hAgeyE bareyabahu.

## viShaya 2 — kannaDa baraha caritra mattu samskxta eravalu

*bhAgagaLu 4–9*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 4–9 →](../kn/full#part-4)

> *(bhAgagaLu 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 kannaDavagalilla adarE bhAga 9 svalpa dorakuttade)*

kannaDa lipi brAmI lipiyinda beLedubandidu. brAmI lipi uccAraNa ADipAya mElE 5 varga vyanjanakoSTa mADide: ka-varga, ca-varga, Ta-varga, ta-varga, pa-varga. I vaiJJAnika rIti. adarE pratikoSTadalli alphaprANa mattu mahAprANa eraDu bErE sAligaLu — I eraDanEya sAlE kannaDadalli avaSyakavE alla.

kannaDa baraha sumAru 2000 varSagaLa hindE ArambavAyitu. A kALada vidvAnsaru samskxtavE ellA JJAnada mULa ennuva nambike hoDiddaru — adaridindu brAmI-samskxta lipi vyavastE purarAyitu. I punaranuvartane neDeyidu hoyi hoyi Idu takka kannaDakku hOLike alla.

ingliS eravalu padagaLu kannaDakke bandAga kannaDigaru "vaT" mattu "vaT" (watch mattu vote) eraDannu "v" ninda bareyuttAre — I nyAyave samskxta eravalu padagaLigU naDeyabEku. "bAratu", "darma", "baLisu" — I rIti bareyuvudu tappalla, sari AguturuttaDE.

baraha sumAru 31 akSaragaLu sAku — kannaDa svantada padakoSada mAtannu pugisi bareyalu. heccina akSaragaLu mahAprANa mattu SakAra mattu visarga — ivugaLu samskxtada kANike; kannaDada svantada avaSyakata alla.

## viShaya 3 — bErE bhAShe gaLu mADida baraha suDAraNe

*bhAgagaLu 10–13*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 10–13 →](../kn/full#part-10)

> *(bhAgagaLu 10, 12, 13 kannaDavagalilla; bhAga 11 svalpa dorakuttade)*

jagattinalli halavAru bhAShe gaLu yaSasvi baraha suDAraNe mADidave — I neDetu sADya embudu sAbitisuttave:

**kOriya:** suDAraNe nantara baraha-mAtu hatyantara kaDimeyAyitu. kOriya makkaLu okkobbaru naDavanige baraha kaLiyuvAga heccina kAlasa-hOlike sADyavAyitu.

**panjAbi (gurmuki lipi):** suDAraNe nantara panjAbi baraha-mAtu haccina hOlikeyalli bareyabahudu sADhyavAyitu.

**inDOnEShiya:** Hague / Spelling suDAraNe nantara (Dutch ninda innA rIti baraha), innUsaru sUlabavAyitu.

**jarmani:** sarkar baraha niyama badalAyisitu — makkaLige baraha kalisuvudu sUlabavAyitu.

**turkI:** lATin lipi ayke nantara baraha-mAtu hOlike hecca AyItu.

**AsAmi (assamese):** svalpa suDAraNe nantara AsAmi baraha-mAtu hOlike sudhAritu.

I ellA udAharaNegaLu tOruttave: baraha suDAraNe sADya; makkaLige sUlabavAguttade; bhAShe hALaguvudilla.

## viShaya 4 — suDAraNe edurukegaLu mattu uttaragaLu

*bhAgagaLu 14–16*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 14–16 →](../kn/full#part-14)

> *(bhAgagaLu 14, 15, 16 kannaDavagalilla — mUla paThya dorakalavillDa)*

suDAraNe edurukegaLige DNS baT avaru eraDu-nAlku uttaragaLannu munnurisuttAre:

**"eraDu pada ondE tara OduttAve — gondala Aguttade":** gondala bahaLa varSa-sandarbhagaLalli sammugha paristhitiyalli oDeDisikkoLLuturuttaDE. mAtinalli E rIti gondala nityavU iruttade — cintisi ODalAGuttade. kannaDa-ingliS koSagaLalli kUDa halavAru pada halavAru arthagaLannu koNDiruttave.

**"2000 varSagaLa sampradAya kaLedukoLLuttade":** sampradAya sari yenbudu pariKSisabEku — haLeyadu embudE suDAraNegE aDcaNe Agabarhadu. lipi oru sAdhana; pUjyada vastu alla.

**"heccina akSaragaLu — heccina samxddhi":** bhASheya samxddhige lipiyannu akSaragaLa sanKyeyanu nODabArade — mAtannu nODabEku. ingliSinalli sumAru 26 akSaragaLu; kannaDadalli 31 akSaragaLu sAku — samxddhige yAva toDakkeyU illa.

**"samskxta mElariMe illiddare?":** samskxta inDo-yUrOpiyan bhAShe; kannaDa drAviDa bhAShe. eraDu bErE bhAShe kuTumba. samskxta tandarige kannaDa magu alla.

**"uccAraNe pratiDoddu bErE":** suDAraNe pratiDoddu uccAraNeyanu oDAnavAdAga sAruvudilla — bahujanara uccAraNe kannaDigarige nityavU hEge AguturuttadO hAgeyE bareyuvudu.

## viShaya 5 — sAmAjika nyAya mattu samAropti

*bhAgagaLu (P-series mattu 15–17)*

[📼 bhAgagaLu 15–17 →](../kn/full#part-15)

> *(bhAgagaLu 15, 16, 17 kannaDavagalilla — mUla paThya dorakalavillDa)*

I pustakada mUla mAthu — baraha suDAraNe sAmAjika nyAyada viShaya:

mElvarga makkaLu maneyalli samskxta eravalu padagaLa sari baraha nityavU kELuttAre — mAlI kELade adi kaLiyuttAre. keLavarga makkaLige I rIti maneyalli kELuva avakASa illa — shAlEyalli kaLisuvudu sADya adarE kaTTa hecca. I eraDu vargagaLa naDuve baraha-kaTTa vyatyAsa heccaguttade.

kUDubale (internet) kALadalli baraha-oduva sAmartyavE jIvana nadavalige mUla-janAnga. lEKa-kAnuka vyavahAra, mAhiti paDeyuvudu, rAjakIya bAgi-tanana — idu ellA baraha bEku. yAru odi bareyalAra avaru innobbara mElE avalambisabEku — idu sAmAjika zameNE (disempowerment).

karnATakadalli naDesuvAga sarvara sAkSaratvakke 2060 varSa takka kALa bEku ennisuttade. baraha suDAraNeyindindu I samaya kaDimeyAgabahu.

eraDu mUrta suDAraNe viShayagaLu:
1. samskxta eravalu padagaLanu kaDime mADabEku — drAviDa mUlada svantada padagaLannu baLasabEku
2. uLida eravalu padagaLannu kannaDigaru hEge uccAraNe mADuttArO hAgeyE bareyabEku

I eraDu badalAvaNe kannaDa makkaLige baraha kalisuvudu sUlabavAgisuttade — eLLara kannaDigarige baraha talaupuvante mADuvudu sAmAjika nyAya.

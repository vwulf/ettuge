## adhyAya 1 — munnOTa
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

### 1.1 kannaDa baraha ellara sottu AgabEku

kannaDa baraha mELvargadavara sottu Agi mAtravE uLiyadE ellara sottu AgabEku mattu kannaDa barahada baLakeyA mUlaka ivattu mELvargadavaru mAtravE paDeyuttiruvA halavu rItiyA prayOjanagaLannu ellA vargada kannaDigarU samAnavAgi paDeyuvA hAgAgabEku emba kaLakaLiyinda nAnu I pustakavanu bareyalu horTiddEnE.

kannaDa baraha ellara sottu AgabEkiddalliA nAvu mADabEkAgiruvA kelasgaLu halavivu. ellA kannaDigarU OdaLu-bareyaLu kaliyuvante mADabEkembudu ivugaLalli modalanevadu.

### 1.2 barahavanu ellarU yAke kaliyabEku?

barahavanu kalitiruvavrA ivattu adariMda entaha prayOjanagaLannu ellavanu paDeyuttiddArE embudanu svalpa vivaravAgi pariSIlisidEvAdalli adanu ellarU kaliyuvA avaSyakatE yAke idE embudu spaSTabAdiradu.

### 1.3 kannaDakke samskxta mUlavalla

kannaDa nuDiya mUla dAvidaDa nuDigaLu — tamila, teLugu, maLayALa — ivugaLallide; samskxtadallilla. I viShayada dRuDhavAda sAdanEyu 2nEya adhyAyadalli koDalAgide.

### 1.4 lipikriAnti: akSharegaLa eNikeyannu kaDime mADuvudu

ivattu nAvu baLasuttiruvA kannaDa barahadalli ayvattu akSharegaLive! kannaDadavEya padagaLannu bareyalu mOvattOndu akSharegaLidrE sAku. samskxtadinda eravalagiA padagaLannu kannaDadalli uccarisuva hAgeye bareyuvA badalu samskxtadalliruvA hAgeyE bareyabEku emba anavaSyakavAda nirbandadindAgi I rIti bEkAdudakkintalU hEcu akSharegaLannu kannaDa barahagaLalli baLasabEkAguttide.

### 1.5 padakriAnti: samskxta padagaLannu kaDime mADuvudu

eraDaneyadu: baraha lipiyannu sudhArisuvuduA mAtravalla — samskxtadinda tegedukONDa padagaLannu kaDime mADi, kannaDadavEya padagaLannu hEcu hEcu baLasabEku.

### 1.6 ajnAnadindAguva anAhutgaLu

nuDi-baraha vyatAsada arivu illAdiruvudu — nuDi mattu barahavanu onDEnE emba tappukkalpanE — halavu anAhutgaLigE kAraNavilide: pUjyavAda SUddhIkaraNa prayatna, araGava lippi sudhAranEgaLu, mauDhyavAda ariSaTvArgadvesha.

## adhyAya 2 — kannaDa nuDi samskxtadinda beLedA bantilla
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

kannaDa nuDigaLa mUla dAvidaDa nuDigaLalli; samskxtadalli alla. I viShayada vijnAnika sAdanE nuDigaLa naDuvina sambandhavaripameyA mUlaka sAdhyavAgide.

**mukhya tiruLu (2.5):** kannaDa mattu samskxtada naDuve pada-guchChagaLalli hatta kuritAdaru, vyAkaraNada naDuvina vyatyAsagaLu tIravAgivE. kannaDa tamILinannu hoLeyuttade; samskxtavannu alla.

## adhyAya 3 — kannaDadalli spelling samasye
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

*bAga ondu: lipikriAnti*

**mUla samasye:** kannaDa baraha samskxta padagaLannu samskxtadalli baredante baredukOLLabEku emba niyama AnusarisuttadE — uccAraneyariya baredukOLLabaradu. idu akSharegaLa saMkhyeyanu 31rinda 50kke hEcisuttade.

**hOlikeya viSlEShaNe:** tamila, maLayALa, panjAbi, sindi ivugaLu samskxta eravalugaLannu bErE bErE rItiyalli kaiyALuttave — heccina bAga uccAranAnusArivAgi. ingliShu baraha kUDA anitya (§3.4.1).

**tiruLu (3.6):** padagaLannu uccarisuva hAgeye bareyiri — samskxta padagaLu kannaDa nuDiyalli agaLeyE baddAgivu.

## adhyAya 4 — kannaDakkOndu 'hosa baraha'
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

**hosa barahada svarUpa (4.2):**
- mahAprANAkSharegaLu (4.2.1): kha→ka, gha→ga, chha→ca, jha→ja, Tha→Ta, Dha→Da, tha→ta, dha→da, pha→pa, bha→ba — 10 akSharegaLu taggisutti
- anuSvAra (4.2.2): nasal+vyanjana
- xa, Sha, visarga (4.2.4): hIsaLutti
- rakArada mUru rUpagaLu (4.2.5): ondu rUpadalli SAdi

**mAtu mattu baraha (4.3):**
mAtu modalu, baraha anantara (4.3.1). lipiyannu vijnAnikateyinda nirmisuvu (4.3.2). mAtannu anunarisuvA baraha satya baraha (4.3.5).

**mukhya tiruLu:** hosa baraha oru Aykeyugurvudu — nirdESavalla.

## adhyAya 5 — kannaDa barahada padasamasye
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

*bAga eraDu: padakriAnti*

## adhyAya 6 — kannaDa barahagaLalli samskxta padagaLu
[↑ oLapiDige hintirugi](#oLapiDi)

viviDa baraha-vargagaLalli samskxta padagaLa daTTateyA pramANa-ADArita sarvEkShaNa. dinapatrikegaLu, janapriya vijnAna baraha, vayaskara SikShaNa — ivugaLalle samskxta daTTatE Ella heccide. I daTTatE heccAda baraha, janara bAhuttakke bEkAda baraha.

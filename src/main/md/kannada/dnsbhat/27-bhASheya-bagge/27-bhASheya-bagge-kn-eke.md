## bhAga ondu — bhASheya svarUpa

### 1.1 yAvu bhAShe mElu yA kILu alla {#11}

bhAShegaLige nAvu "saraLa", "kaSTa", "sogasAda", "garaTu", "ADi-bArA", "beLeda" antella viSeSaNagaLannu koLLuttEve — Adare nuDiyarime (linguistics) sam-sOdane ee guNagaLu bhAShegaLa svantakke sErida viSayagaLalla endu tOrisuttade. ivella keLabEvaru beLedukOnDu banda vaiyaktika/mAnasika nOTa-buDi-kaLe mAtragaLu. bhAShe hEge huttittu embude kaLiyuvavarige Arambada bhASheyE saraLa; ingindu nODida aTTa kannaDigaLu cInIyannu kaLiyuvudu kannaDiganige kannaDa kaLiyuvudu eTTu kaSTavO aTTu kaSTa. samskxtakke duSTa vyAkaraNa idE, Adare A kaSTatu "vyAkaraNa varNane"dalli iddE hordadu, samskxtannu tAy-nuDigE kandu kanDu beLeda jana samskxtannu kaliyadE idu sADisvada. bhASheyarimeyavannu ondu bhASanu aTTininda aTTannu prAmANita vargIsalu mADida prayatnagaLella viphalavAdE bAdave. ondu amsa (pada-racane) saraLAvAga, innondu amsa (vAkya-racane) kaSTAvAguttade; muttavAgi bhAShegaLu santulanavAgi biDuttave. bhAShe-ShreNIkrama (linguistic hierarchy) embude illa.

### 1.2 bhAShegaLa huTTu mattu sAvugaLu {#12}

bhAShegaLu huTTuttave, badalAguttave, savuttave — Adare jIvigaLa rItiyanalla. ondu bhAShe sAvudu andare adara ellA janaru bEre bhASheyondige hOguttAre; ondu bhAShe huTTudu andare gAmphaLya eraDu bhASheyAgi oru janarinda ondu janarindondu artha-tODAdantAyitu. baT avaru mAnava-bhAShE huTTida mondina rahasiyu gambhIravAda maTTakke hAgE UhadE idE endu tOrisuttAre — sidda bhAShegaLa huTTu (kannaDigaLa huTTu dravIDiya sam-sOdanadinda kANabahudAga) bhAShA-sAmarThya emba mAnava-vaisistya huTTida rahasiginta bEre — adannu driDha-sthirapaDisi hELalAguvudilla. nibandha bArate bhAShegaLa nAsu (extinction), sAyuva bhAShegaLu, mattu bhAShA-vividhate endare mAnava-samskxtikke endare embadannu vivisuttade.

### 1.3 bhAShegaLalli hata mattu hosa — bhAShe-badalu {#13}

ellA badukunuva bhAShegaLu nittya badalaAguttave — ucchAraNe, pada-kOsa, vyAkaraNa talE-talE badalaAguttave. baT avaru kannaDa upabhAShegaLinda idu tOrisuttAre: karaLa kannaDada Havyaka upabhAShe kenda kannaDigaLu kaLedukOnDa konDu Hata-kannaDa lakSaNagaLannu (-andiru anta bahuvacana oTTannu uLasikonDu) uLisikOnDirE, A prade-shadalli hosa lakSaNagaLannu tande-tande beLesikonDiddE. badalu "hALu" alla; adu avoidable, anivArya, niyama-baddha natanE. "Suddha" bhAShe embude mithya; bhAShe sthira-niranta iruvudu enda kAlavu irlilla.

### 1.4 bhAShA-svAtantrya {#14}

bhAShegaLu formal-tarka-parimitiyanalli kaTTubiTTa alla — avugaLalli svAtantrya mattu sampradAya ondE sArigirivuvu. jagattinalli yAvu nuDiyariru bEkAdaru mundu kELadE iru vAkyagaLannu sxSTisalAguvudu; makkaLu karpisidarida niyamagaLannu tiLidukOnDu bhAShegaLa vyAkaraNannu hode-hode tiLidukOnDe beLeyuttAre. bhASheya ee sxjana-svAtantrya (baT "bhAShA-svAtantrya" embadu) nirdista bhAShE-vAkya-niyamagaLinda bErE viSaya. bhAShegaLu linga-racaneyanalli, kAla-kanTegaLalli, encuva paddhatiyanalli bEre bEre AgiruvudarE "yAvaduE sOdilAda yA tarkA-bhAvadadu" endarvudalla.

### 1.5 bhAShe tarkabaddavE? {#15}

"bhAShe tarkabadda alla" endu janaru hala-ganTe dUridu-dUridu hELuttAre — ingliSudi "I went" (not "I goed") yAke? konDu bhAShegaLalli double negation yamannu gooNisuttadE yAke? baT tOrisuttAre: bhAShe tavaru antaranga-tarkavannu anusarIte, adu formal-tarkaginta bErAgirubahudu. linga-racane (kannaDa jIvi/nijIvi bEdadalli, not pum/strI bEda), vacana-vividhate (konDu bhAShegaLalli ondu, eraDu, mUru, nAlku entu vibhAgagaLu), mattu kAla-vibhAga (ellA bhAShegaLu onEE tarka-kAlAnnu guri-paduvudilla) kurita udAharaNagaLannu koDuttAre. ee vividhatagaLu tarka-kampavalla — bhasitannu kaDeyuva bEre bEre mArgagaLu.

### 1.6 kAla (vartamAna kAla) {#16}

"vartamAna kAla" endu nAvu embuvadannu nibandha oru kroDI-viSayavAgi upayOgisi bhAShe-dATE kAlika-vividhate tOrisuttade. kannaDigaLalli nAvu "vartamAna kAla" embadu ingliSinadu ginta joTilavu: iru-kriyeyondige Agalade kArya (present moment), abhyAsa (habitual present), sAmAnya satya endu bEre bEre. Manipuriyalli kAla anivArya-baddavAgi tiLisalAguvudu; cIniyalli kriyA-padagaLu kAlakke baddalAguvudilla. "vartamAna kAla" endu vyAkaraNakAraru hELuvudu bEre bEre bhAShegaLalli bEre bEre bageya kAlika-artha koDuttadE endu nibandha tOrisuttade.

### 1.7 encuvudu kUDa bEre bEre bhAShegaLalli bEre bEre AgiruttadE {#17}

jagattina bhAShegaLalli encu-paddhati natana-nATakIya vividhateyondu tOrisuttade. halavuvantu bhAShe-10 upayOgisuttave; konDu bhAShe-4, bhAShe-12, bhAShe-20 upayOgisuttave. encina padagaLannondige hesaru-pada-vargIkaraNa (classifiers / mELapagaLu) anivArya mADuva bhAShegaLu bErE ive — kannaDigaLu jIvigaLige "-aru" mattu vastugaLige "-u" antAhavugaLannu upayOgisuttAre. konDu bhAShegaLu ellA hesaru-pada-kantegaLallu vacana-nirdista anivArya; inDoralli adu ananivArya. ee vividhate kUDa — encuvudu eTTu sArvaTrIka endu tOruttu — samskxti mattu bhAShika sampradAyavirali rUpagolLuttadE endu tOrisuttade.

## bhAga eraDu — vyAkaraNa mattu kalika

### 2.1 mAtru-bhAShe kaLeyuva mAyA {#21}

makkaLu huTTida 2–3 varSagaLalliyadarE tama mAtru-bhASheyalli pUrNa hidiTagaLannu sADisutte — ee sAdhane AnyabAse kaLiyuva Daige prauDharantaha neraLinda sulabha mADalAguvudalla. baT avaru "tIkSaNa-kAlavAdha" (critical period) ippaTannu vivEcisuttAre: makkaLige sumbAru aidu-Aru varSagaLa varege viSeSa bhAShE-kaLike-sAmarthya Agalade saktiyAgi irutte, nantara kaDimAgutte. indE AyAsapadE eraDu-mUru bhAShegaLannu kaliyabahudu embadu konDu bhAShegaLalli makkagaLu sADisuttAre. kannaDa makkaLu kaLiyuvudu endu bEkAguvudu endu kELiddu-kElada ella eDali-vyAkaraNannu kanDupiDiyutte embade ee nibandha vivEcisuttade, yAke embade bEke niddEya-sandEhavE uLidakOmbatte aDagide.

### 2.2 kannaDa makkaLige kannaDa karpisu {#22}

karnATakadalli nUdoLu kannaDa tAy-nuDiyanavarige kannaDa karpuvagalu adiguru-kAlattim modalE kaLiyuttAru embante — vyAkaraNa-niyama-paTTi, vibakti-pATha, tappa-tirupisuva abhyAsagaLa mUlaka. baT ee paddhati tappu endu vAdisutte: makkaLige mAtuvannaDE kannaDa iddE; nUdoLu mADabEkAdudE bAraha-sAmarThya mattu grama-sthara-vibheddagaLannu beLesikoLLudu, avara svAbhAvika mAtuvannu "tirupisuvadittu". makkaLu nUdoLige teruva mAtu mattu avaru bareyabEkAda baraha naDuva abaddhakke suttu-variya yocanE-sUkshmacintanE bEku endu nibandha hELuttade; adakke bEku adhika vyAkaraNa-DrillAgalla.

### 2.3 vyAkaraNa — kalliNa kaDale? {#23}

"kalliNa kaDale" andare cUttige tinnagAgi kANuttade Adare tinnalavAguvudalla enda kannaDa nANNuDi. baT nUdoLu kannaDigaLalli karpuva vyAkaraNa intahude endu vAdisutte. SAlA-vyAkaraNagaLu bhASheyanu samskxta-vargagaLalli varisuttave (nAmapada/hesaru pada, kriyApada/esaka pada itara) — ee vargagaLu kannaDa hEgE kAryanirvahistade embadannu sariyadAgi varisuttavalla. svAbhAvikavAgi kannaDigaLu anusarisu niyamagaLannu karputtave, kannaDigaLannu satyanAgi racisi biDuva niyamagaLannu tapputtave. pariNAma: makkaLu adhika barehuve, heccAgi oDuttAre yA bhAShe-maramU arthavAttAgi tiLiyuttAre embante vyAkaraNa-kalika sahAya mADuvudilla.

### 2.4 nUdoL-baraha-pATemadina modalina pATha {#24}

baT konDu pramANita kannaDa SAlA-vyAkaraNa pustakadinaduva modalina adhyAyannu viSlESisi, ellA pustakadinaduvu "bhAShe" endare endu anta suttuvELEyanalli yA tappudAriyalli vivarisi, nantara tiTTanE samskxta-mUla pada-vargagaLa paTTi sErisuttave endu kANuttAre. ee modalina pATha tappu chApAnnu taDeyutte: kannaDigaLa racane samskxtada racaneyannu kANisuttade embantE, intalla. baT satiyanAda modalina pATha entiruttade — kannaDigaLu nita nijavaAgi mAtanADuvAgala mattEnu mADuttAre emba AdhAradi uttaru koLLuva ondu pATha — endU hELuttAre.

### 2.5 kannaDa vyAkaraNadalli samskxtada baralu {#25}

kannaDa vyAkaraNa varNaneyalla pramANita paramparE — kEsirAjara SabdamaNi darpaNavarE hidi 20nEya nUrAdina pATimudraNadavarege — samskxta vyAkaraNa-vargagaLannu anuvartisuttade. idu sariyadalla endu baT tOrisuttAre yAkendare kannaDa ondu dravIDiya bhAShe-simhu adara khadyOtakakke mUlate-bEre rItiyanalla racane iDuvudu: kannaDa samskxtadintakannaDa-rItyalli linga-bEda illE; kriyA-vyavasthE bEre rItiyalli kAryanirvahistade; jODupada-racane bEre niyamagaLannu anusarisuttade. "samskxtada baralu" kannaDa vyAkaraNada naDuvE iTTiruvarale kEndrabhAvadaDi varNisuttade, hattara kannaDigaLa nija-racaneyannu virUpagOLisuttade.

### 2.6 kannaDigaLalli samskxta samAsagaLu iveyE? {#26}

samskxtakke vikasitagonDa samAsa-vargIkaraNa irutte: tatpuruSa, dvandva, bahuvrIhi, avyayibhAva, karmadharAya. pUrANa kannaDa vyAkaraNakAraru ee tatte kannaDa jODupadagaLige tattaruttAre. baT ee tatte sariyadA endu parISilisi, adhikabaluge tattaruvudilla endu kANuttAre. kannaDa jODupadagaLu bEre rItiyanalla — adhikabaluge kEndra-bindu bala amsa (right-headed), idu samskxta jODupadagaLa sArvaTrika lakSaNa alla. nibandha kannaDa-svagata jODupada-vargIkaraNagaLannu beLeyuva pakSadalli battisuttade.

### 2.7 kannaDa makkaLige ingliSu karpisu {#27}

karnATakadinadA SAlA-vyavasthE ingliSu-kalikanalla tumbA kAla viniyOgisuttade; Adare adhikabalagu vidyArtHigaLu ingliSannu sariyadAgi upayOgisi hOragE baruvudilla. baT rogadannu patteyItte: ingliSannu kannaDa vyAkaraNa-mUlaka (kannaDiginta racane-rItyalli bEre), paDadali-akSaravaLi kaLita-kaLita, mattu kELugAgi badalA oDugAgi karputtEve. ingliSu-bhAShA-kalika, ingliSu-sAhitya-kalikaninda bEre mADabEku endU, sam-sOdanEdina uttama eraDanEya-bhAShA-kalika paddhatiyannu (uccaraNE-kaLika, samvAda-abhyAsa) vyAkaraNa-anuvAda-paddhatiginta bEre upayOgisabEku endU baT SifArisMaDuttAre.

### 2.8 bhAShe kalikalalli vyAkaraNada sahAya {#28}

vyAkaraNa tiLivudu bhAShA-kalikanalle sahAya mADuttadE? baT sakAra-nikAra iru amsagaLannu beLakannu koDuttAre: tAy-nuDi beLavaNigeyalli, spaSta vyAkaraNa adhikabalagu anivArya alla — makkaLu sariyadAgi mAtanADuvudu kaLiyuttAre adilla. eraDanEya-bhAShA-kalikanalli, konDu vyAkaraNa-jnAna upayOgAda (kaLiyuttiruvudannu kanTa-kaTTabahudu), Adare vyAkaraNa samvAdakke sEvalAguva upakaraNa irAbEku, gatEyAguva lakSya irAbAradu. nibandha kannaDa SAlEgaLu indinA kalpiyuva sahAyaginta adhika vyAkaraNannu kalpisi biDuttirutte endu sam-sOdana parizIlisi tOrisuttade.

### 2.9 ondu ganTeyalli eTTu paTagaLu ODuttIri? {#29}

kannaDa ODuvAga sAmarThyavantu kannaDa janaru OduvAga daram-naTTa mADuttAre — akSara-jnAna iruvavaru pustakagannu ODuvudilla. baT kAraNagaLu viSlESisuttAre: mAtu mattu baraha kannaDatanDu AbhEda (baraha samskxta-prabAvita, hIgAgi kammi paricita), lipi-joTilate (aTTu-paTTagaLu artidukOLLa bEkAda eraDu-aksarE-kUTugaLu), sAma-sAdhAraNa-oDuva viSaya-sAhitya badavuttana, mattu mAtu karagaDAgilagi ucci baraha-agiru samskxtigaLa pratishThE. oDuva daravu heccisu bEkendare lipi-saraLI-karaNa mattu dina-kannaDadalli heccu prApta-sAhitya tOrisuva pakSadalli battisuttade.

### 2.10 ODalAguvAga kUDa janaru yAke ODuvudilla? {#210}

akSara-jnAna iruva kannaDigaLu kUDa anivAryadE heccAgi kanDu ODuvudilla. baT sAmAjika mattu samskxtika kAraNagaLannu viSlESisuttAre: hala kannaDa mane-mane OduvuDu kAlakSEpa-caTuvaTike alla; saarvajanakapalabante sAhitya biDuvuDu; sadxSya-sambhAvana-kaDime baraha-vilasi-sAhitya-durlabanavartana; mattu akSara-jnAna-anivArya-illade tElivisida rEDiyO-dUradarSana viyOjanagaLu. nibandha lipi-sarpaDike mattu uttama SAlA-paddhatiyo ondige kannaDa-oDuva-samskxti-caLuvaLi tODisuvudu pakSadalli battisuttade.

## bhAga mUru — mAtu mattu baraha

### 3.1 mAtina citra {#31}

baraha mAtina "citra" — dxSya-sancAradi mAtu-bhASheyannu huDikitu-muTTisuva oru mArgE. sDpEkTragrAm (dvani-tarangagaLa dxSya dhAKalE) bhAShe-nijavaagi embadannu sUcisuttade endu vivisuttade. baT nantara kaDeEntu mUru purAtana samskxtigaLalli — ijipta, cIna, mattu MAyA — svatamindriyavAgi baraha-sxSTiyAyitu embadannu vivarisi, baraha-paddhatiyanalla eraDu mukhya bageya — logographic (ondu aksarake ondu pada; cIna-aksaragaLu) mattu phonographic (ondu aksarake ondu dvani; akSaramAle yA kannaDa-akSaramAle) embadannu vivEcisuttAre.

### 3.2 bhAratakke ondE lipi bEkE? {#32}

"rASTriya-aikya" emba udde-saraNe bhAratadalli ondE lipi ellA bhAShegaLige andare embu praSTeyannu baT viSlESisi nirakkarisuttAre. avaru parASIlisu mukhya vAdigaLu: (a) ondE lipi bEre prade-Sikarannu uttama samparkisuttade — baT ippa lipi-jnAna bEre bEre bhAShegaLannu ArEyuvaru endu gottu-vadannu sElisuttAre; lipi tiLidu bEre bhASheyannu aritu biDuvudilla. (b) sAmAnya lipi samskxtika AvEga tOrisuttade — baT lipi sAhityika mattu samskxtika guri-niraUpaNegE aniSyuta-sambandha endu uttarIsuttAre. sarpaDike sAdisu sAriytAtmaka phalavailla, kelata sAhitya-paramparEgaLannu nAmsEvudu endu mugisutte.

### 3.3 Ra-kArada (ಋ) uLidukoLLuvudu {#33}

samskxta svara *ṛ* (ಋ) mAtu-kannaDigaLalli sangata dvani-dhAra illa — kannaDigaLu adannu "ri" (ರಿ) endu uccarisuttAre. Adare samskxta-eravalu padagaLige kannaDa baraha-paddhatiyanalli adu uLisikOnDiruttade. baT idu anivArya-vE endu praSIlisuttAre. alla embu sankhyE koDuttAre: samskxta-eravalu padagaLannu kannaDa dvanigaLalli uccarisuttEve, hIgAgi avuvannu kannaDa akSaragaLalli barehuvudu sariyadE. ಋ aksara kevaLa padada samskxta-mUla gurtisuttade — A kArya oru niganTu-tippaNeyannu mADabahudagillade kaliyanuyuku makkaLige oru viSEsa aksara-smaraNeyE avashyavilla.

### 3.4 tarkiyA lipi krAnti {#34}

1928ralli tarkiyA arabi-lipiyanu (Ottoman Turkishge upayOgisuttiddadu) LaTin-mUla akSaramAlEyondige badaliside — onde talE-mAruge sAmAnya-jnAna adhika-prAya sADisitu. baT ee aitihAsika udAharaNavannu upayOgisi lipi-sarpaDike sADyavA mattu phalakArivA endu tOrisuttAre. tarkiyA prakaraNavannu tOrisuttade: (a) rAjakIya icChA-bala iruvAgala onde talE-mAruge pUrNa lipi-badalu sADyavAguttade; (b) sAhityika-paramparE nAmsuvudilla (adannu hosa-lipigE parivartisubahudAdu); mattu (c) baraha-vyavastheyA saraLI-karaNa nATakIya akSaratA-labdhiyannu taDedukoDuttade. kannaDa lipi-sarpaDikeya pakSadalli ee udAharaNavannu bEre bEre kaDeyalli hOlisutte.

### 3.5 kannaDigaLige "hosa baraha" {#35}

mUranesarha adhyAyagaLa mEle badisi ee nibandha bhAga-mUrana kEndriya nibandha. baT avaru "hosa baraha" endu ambuvudannu kaDeyuttAre: idu kannaDa-aksaramAlEyinda hattu mabAprANa-aksaragaLannu (Ka, Ga, Ca, Ja, Ta, Da, Ta, Da, Fa, Ba), retrOphlex sibilant (Sa), mattu bEre konDu samskxta-eravalu padagaLige tattalAguvavE hordadu kannaDigaLalli phOnOlOjikala kArya mADada konDu aksaragaLannu taLLedubiDuvudu. avarA vAdu: mUla kannaDa padagaLu ee dvanigaLannu viSiSTavAgi upayOgisuvudilla; ee aksaragaLu kevaLa eravalu padagaLa samskxta-barehugE uLisikOnDiruttave. aksaramAlEyannu 31–34 aksaragaLige (indinA 49+dinda) saraLisuvadale kalika-bArannu kaDimE mADi akSaratEyannu heccisuttade.

### 3.6 bhAShe mattu baraha {#36}

mAtu (ಮಾತು) mattu baraha (ಬರಹ) naDuvina bEdavanu kanTuvAda niBandha. mAtu mUlabhUta: ellA janaru mAtanADuttAre; baraha nantara banda, samskxtika kanDupidi. mAtu nittya badalaAguttade; baraha sthitiSIla. kalitu baraha pratiSThE mAtu-rUpagaLige "kILarime" yA upabhAShegaLige "kILarime" sxSTisuttade. mAtina prAthamikate gurtisuvudu kannaDa akSaratA, SikSaNa, mattu mAnakI-karaNa embu viSayagaLalli namma cintane hEge badalaAgabEku embadannu tOrisuttade.

## bhAga nAlku — kannaDa bhASheya svarUpa

### 4.1 kannaDigaLa mUru mukha {#41}

kannaDa kaDeEntu mUru bEre-bEre rUpagaLalli irutte: (a) dina-jIvitada mAtu-upabhAShe(gaLu), pradesha, jAti, talE-mArugU bErE; (b) baraha-rUpada mAnaka (grantha kannaDa), sthiracitta mattu samskxta-prabAvita; mattu (c) hala prade-Sha mattu sAmAjika upabhAShegaLu (Havyaka, koDavu, guLLa, TuLu-prabAvita karaLa-kannaDa, baDaga, itara). baT mUru "mukhagaLu" ella bhAShika-paddhatiyanalli onE-onE tyaka-samAnagaLu endu vAdisutte. baraha-mAnaka mattu mAtu-sAmAjika-rUpagaLa naDuvina pratiSThA-AbhEda sAmAjika utpadane, bhAShika satya alla.

### 4.2 kannaDigaLa upabhAShegaLu {#42}

kannaDigaLa pramukha upabhAShe-gumpugaLa vistarita sarvEkSaNe. baT mukhya vibhAjanagaLannu varisuttAre: karaLa upabhAShegaLu (Havyaka, TuLu-prabAvita bEdagaLu), kEndrIya puTa-upabhAShegaLu, uttarA upabhAShegaLu (adhika urdU/hindi prabAva iruva), mattu dakSiNa upabhAShegaLu (adhika tamiLu prabAva iruva). upabhAShegaLu bedisuvudu lakSaNagaLannu hELuttAre: pada-kOSa, ucchAraNe, mattu konDu vyAkaraNa-lakSaNagaLu (bahuvacana-nirdharaNe). kannaDigaLa prasAranUda itihAsavannu mattu adannu koDida sAmAjika-gumpugaLannu upabhAShe-bEdagaLu pratibimbisutte embu nibandha okkaT TippaNi mADuttade — bhAShika "ahita" alla.

### 4.3 kannaDigaLa vyAkaraNa paramparE {#43}

kannaDa vyAkaraNa-barEyuva itihAsAvAda sarvEkSaNe — uLidiruvalla mattu haLeyada vyAkaraNa kEsirAjara SabdamaNi darpaNa (~1260 CE) dinda hiDidu briiTiSu-kAlada vyAkaraNagaLinda hidi 20nEya nUrAdinda varege. baT ellA paramparEyu samskxta vyAkaraNa-caukaTTinalli kAryanirvahistade endu tOrisuttAre. kAlina kannaDa vyAkaraNakAraru avara vyAkaraNagaLannu samskxtadalli yA kannaDa-samskxta-sankara-rUpadi baresi, spaStagonDu pANinIya-vyAkaraNava namUne mADidarU. ee pratishThA-paramparE kannaDigaLa racaneya Shatamanasankhya virUpagaLannu sxSTisidE.

### 4.4 oru nUru varSagaLa kannaDa vyAkaraNa sam-sOdane {#44}

19nEya nUrAdina kelavindA kalpitage briiTiSu aDikarArigaLu (KiTTeL, Rice), yurOpina dravIDa-jnAnigaLu (CaldveL, ZvElEbil), mattu bharatIya jnAnigaLu (B.D. SatyAnArAyaNa, T.V. VenkaTacala SaSTri, mattu baT swayam) sEridante kannaDa bhAShika pAnDityannu vivisuttade. baT pratigONAde pratigONu ella aDiyali nATkiyAdadu mattu tappAdadu endu maulyAnkana mADuttAre, samskxta-vargagaLu baDiyA raci-pantaga-paddhatiyannu upayOgisida jnAnigaLu kannaDigaLa adhikasariyAda varNanegaLannu nIDuttAre endu vAdisutte.

### 4.5 doDD samskxta panDitaru tappannu mADidAga {#45}

tIkSaNa-grantha-kAryagaLannu biDugaDe mADida prasiddha kannaDa jnAnigaLu — adhikAravarNanAtmaka uddhAragaLannu sxSTisidavarU — samskxta-vargagaLannu tattariddarinda kannaDa vyAkaraNavannu varisuvagala kramabaddha tappugaLannu mADiala endu baT tOrisuttAre. janara hEsarigE vyAkaraNa-pATemadinda ellA niddEyanIdida tappugaLannu uDaharaNayondige koDuttAre (udA: kriyA-jODupadagaLa tappAda viSlEshaNa, vibakti-oTTugaLa tappAda vargIkaraNa). uddesha vyaktiyanu vikaTisuvudu alla; samskxta-caukaTTu paramparEyanDika taLahake mADida samsthAtmaka ahita tOrisuvadale.

### 4.6 tantrajnAna-padagaLa sxSTi {#46}

vijnyAna, tantrajnAna, mattu adhunika-jIvitakke kannaDa pada-kOsavannu hEge beLesikoLLabEku? baT eraDu sAmAnya mArgagaLannu virOdhisuttAre: (1) samskxta-mUla nava-padagaLu guNaprAya-kaDeyuttuvudu (udA: dUradarSana "television"), anupravEshyavAda mattu prAptyasADyavAda padagaLannu sxSTisuttade; mattu (2) ingliSu padagaLannu samAgra-rUpadi anusarisu, ingliSu-jnAna illAdavarannu (non-English speakers) hErE biDuttade. mUranesarha mArgakke battisuttAre: kannaDigaLa svanta utpAdaka mUla-oTTugaLinda hosa padagaLannu cekkugUdisuvadale (*heserike* "naming/nomenclature", *nuDi-arime* "linguistics", itara). nivandha avara swantada SifAriSu-padagaLannu sErisuttade.

## bhAga aidu — jagattina bhAShegaLu

### 5.1 bhAratada bhAShegaLu {#51}

bhArata jagattina adhika bhAShika-vividhate iruva desha, 700 hEccu bhAShegaLonDige. baT nAlku mukhya bhArata-bhAShe-kuTumbagaLannu sarvEkSisuttAre: dravIDa (kannaDa, tamiLu, teLugu, maLALam, mattu halavAru cinna bhAShegaLu), inDO-AriyA (hindi, bangALi, marATi, gujarAti, itara), TibetO-burma (maNipuri, boDO, mattu EsAnE bharatada hala bhAShegaLu), mattu AusTrO-eshiyAtika (santAli, munDAri, khAsi). "bhAShika kSEtra" (India samAgrata: retroflexion, SOV krama, eravalu-pada-kOsa) viSayavanu vivEcisuttAre. "hindi bhAratada "rASTrabhASA"" emba mithyeyannu virOdhisuttAre — adu kevaLa sumbAru 40% janarige tAy-nuDi.

### 5.2 Esiyada bhAShegaLu {#52}

Esiyada mukhya bhAShe-kuTumbagaLa sarvEkSaNe: SinO-TibeTan (cIni, TibeTan, birma), JapOnik (jApAni), kOrEAN, Altaik (tarkI, mangOliyA, mAncu), sEmITik (arabi, hibrU), IrAniyA (phArsi, pashTO), mattu ekakulA (jApAni, kOrIyan). Esiya jagattinalli adhikajalanu mAtanADuvavara bhAShegaLannu (manDarIna cIni, hindi, arabi) mattu racane-rItyalli adhika viShESavAda konDu bhAShegaLannu (jApAni, adara viSEdavAda gaurava-vyavasthE mattu viSaya-pratikriyA-racane) hoDagisuttade endu baT TippaNi mADuttAre.

### 5.3 yurOpina bhAShegaLu {#53}

inDO-yurOpina kuTumba yurOpinalli pramukha, adara mukhya ShAkhegaLindige: jarmAniya (ingliSu, jarmanu, DaTcu, svEDiSu), rOmAnsu (phrencu, spEniSu, iTeliyana, pOrTugIzu, rOmAniyana), SlavIya (raSiyA, pOliSu, cEku), mattu bErE (grIku, albEniyA, ArmEniyA). yurOpina ekakulA bhAShegaLannu TippaNi mADuttAre: bhAShku (ekakulA), phinniSu mattu eSTOniyana (yurAliku). nijavaagi grIku mattu lATinu yurOpinalli SastrIya bhAShegaLAgi (dakSiNa Esiyalli samskxta kArya nirvahisuvantE) pAtra nirvahistave endU, ingliSu jAgatika samparka-bhAShEyAgi pracalavalidu endU nibandha hELuttade.

### 5.4 Aprikada bhAShegaLu {#54}

Aprika jagattinA adhika bhAShika-vividhate iruva khanD, 2,000 hEccu bhAShegaLonDige. baT nAlku mukhya Aprika kuTumbagaLannu sarvEkSisuttAre: Nijer-kangO (adarinda adhikabalagu doDD, svAhili, zuLu, yOruba sEridante), NilO-sAhArA, Aprō-eshiyAtika (arabi, amhArik, hAusA), mattu kOisAna (adara viSiSTavAda kliku-dvanigaLonDige). Aprikada UDAdina yurOpiya bhAShegaLannu sarkArI-dajE-gE ettida kaLaniya-itihAsavannu, idu ShikSaNa mattu akSaratEge udayavAguva bAdegaLannu, mattu Aprika bhAShe-samudhAyagaLa nittyanAda chaitanyavannu nibandha vivisuttade.

### 5.5 amErikada bhAShegaLu {#55}

amErikagaLa svadEsha bhAShegaLu halavAru bEre bEre bhAShe-kuTumbagaLannu pratiNidhisutte — amErikagaLa modalina janAvasEyA nantara 15,000+ varSagaLa bEre bEre beLavaNigeya phala. mukhya gumpugaLannu (Algonkina, AThabAskan, OTO-mangiyA, kEcuA, TupiyA) vivEcisi, yurOpiya vasahatiya kAraNavAgi bhAShika-nAsu aTTu kaSTakara AgiddE endu TippaNi mADuttAre. spEniSu, pOrTugIzu, ingliSu indu rAjakIyavAgi pramukha Adare hala swadESa bhAShegaLu — kEcuA (80 lakSa mAtanADuvavaru), guarAni (50 lakSa), NAhuATalu (15 lakSa) — balavanThavAgi mAtanADalpaDuttave.

### 5.6 AsTrEliyada bhAShegaLu {#56}

AsTrEliya AbOrijinaL bhAShegaLu (hala nUru) ondu bEre bEre bhAShe-kuTumba-sriNikhalEyannu (pAma-nyungana khanD-vAgE pramukha, uttarAdalli bEre bEre sambandhitAlla kuTumbagaLu). baT vasahatiya kAraNavirali hala AsTrEliya bhAShegaLu inDu apAya-sthitiyalli yA nAshavAgiddE endu TippaNi mADuttAre. AsTrEliya bhAShegaLa ciragina racane-lakSaNagaLannu varisuttAre: hala bhAShegaLu visistadAda sambandha-padagaLannu mattu evidentiality nirdharaNagaLannu (mAtanADuvavaru tiLidukOnDiguvudu hEge embadannu vyAkaraNabaddavAgi anivArya-rUpadi nirdharisuttAre) hoDagisuttave.

### 5.7 OsiyAniyAda bhAShegaLu {#57}

AusTrOnESiya kuTumba, pOlEnESiya (hAvAiyan, MAOri, sAmOa), MElAnESiya, mattu mikrOnESiyA bhAShegaLannu hoDagisuttade, madagAskara (Aprika kincAnE) dinda hAvAi (prashAnta mahAsAgara) varege — yAva bhAShe-kuTumbadinAdarU adhika bhaumika-praSArE. ee viSaya purAtana AusTrOnESiya samudra-yAtrigaLu ee bhAShegaLannu prashAnta-mahAsAgaravirali koDida embadannu hELuttade. papuA nyU gini mattu kUDa hELuttAre, jagattinalli adhika bhAShe-santate iruva desha: ondu deShadalli 800 hEccu bhAShegaLu.

### 5.8 muktAya {#58}

pustaka modalina viSayakke hOguttade: sAmAnya janaru tiLidukOnDiguvudiginta bhASheya bagge kammi tiLidiruttAre, avaru hoDagisiruva tappudAri-grahahagaLu nijavaAgi parIhara-bAdavAda pariNAmagaLannu hoDagisuttave. bhASheyannu vaisnikavAgi tiLidukOLLuvudu — yAvu bhAShe mElu alla embu, ellA bhAShegaLu badalaAguttave embu, baraha mAtinaDiyalli baruttade embu, vyAkaraNa-vividhate mAnava-sampaTTu embu — kannaDigaLu tama bhASheya bagge, karnATakadalli bhAShika alpasankhyAtara bagge, mattu jagattinalli kannaDigaLa sthAnada bagge hEge cintisuttAre embadannu badalaAgabEku. pustaka hEgE mugidate badalAvisuvudu: mandagana bhAvana-cALuvike, polemical kArya-parikalpaneyonDige alla; naru kanDupidi: adigeDe bhASheya bagge spaSTagonDu hELikE tiLiduttade endu bhAvisuvAgala, nuDiyarimeyavannu ondE iru-sAri parizIlisunTe endu sahaja-nimantraNa.

## ellara kannaDa (ellara kannaDa)

"ellara kannaDa" (ellara kannaDa / EK) ennuvudu mAtanADuva mattu bareyuva kannaDa nuDiya naDuvina amtaravannu kaDime mADuva guriyimda mADida munnuDiyAgide. idannu hesarAda nuDiyariga mattu padmaSrI bahumAna geddava Di.en.Samkara bhaT (D.N.Sankara BhaT) munnaDesidaru. ## sAmskxtika nOTa (Cultural perspective)

idu heccAgi mukta sAmskxtika hELikeyAgiddu idu teredu koneyAgabahudu. itareDegaLamte bhAratadalli nuDigaLu jagaLada viShayavAgide.

nuDigaLa bErebEretana, bErebEre dALigaLa himdina kathe, horanADina ALvike, bhAratada eraDu bhAgagaLAguvike, rAjyagaLa nuDi gumpugUDike, paTTaNagaLAguvike mattu bhAratIya cimtaneya mumde taruvikeya ittIcina pravxtti (mattu nuDi mattu dharmada tammadE emba pravxttigaLa jotege) abhiprAyagaLa kaleyannu mUDiside.

nuDigaLa bagge emdinamte homdiruva nOTavu kalviya oppigeyimda sampUrNavAgi bEreyAgide. innomdu badiyalli, nuDi tiLivinalli sAvirAru varShagaLa haLeya vADikegaLive, atyamta balavAdavu pANini mattu pANinIya sollarimeya kalikeya SAlegaLu.

I vADikegaLalli bele iddarU, kannaDa mattu itara drAviDa nuDigaLa sollarimegaLannu ellA horanuDigaLu apabhramSagaLu (athavA bhraShTa mAtu) emdu nambida janaru barediddAre embudannu nAvu gamanadalliTTukoLLabEku. omdE mApina hambaladalli, PIE allada nuDi kuTumbagaLannu pANini horanuDige baLasida adE yOjaneyimda kaliyalAgide.

## gurigaLu mattu guriyalladavugaLu (Goals and non-goals)

Eke yu adara sulabhatana mattu baLakeya sulabhatege mavnavAgi seLeyuvikeyannu nIDida kaTTaleya kannaDada mEle ellara kannaDakke [^49] modala darje nIDuttade. Eke yu HK [^50] namte adE gurigaLannu homdilla. HK yu dEvanAgariyallina mUla kAguNitavannu niShTheyimda uLisikoLLuva guriyannu homdide amdare dEvanAgariyallina mUla kAguNitavannu HK yallina paThyadimda marukaTTabahudu.

Eke yu imgliSh‌na kAguNita samasyegaLannu tiddalu hambalisuvudilla. Arpabet [^51], imgliSh‌gAgi viki hELike [^52] namtaha halavAru paruSagaLiddarU spaShTa oppigeyilla. IPA [^53] sAkaShTu sampUrNavAgide Adare kaliyalu gomdalatanavAgide mattu bEre gurutugaLa agalavAda baLakeyu emdina Desk‌TAp mattu phOn kIbOrD‌gaLalli baLasalu kaShTakaravAgisuttade.

bhAratIya hELikegaLu mattu imgliSh annu omdugUDisalu hambalisuvudu sAkaShTu kaShTakaravAgide. avugaLannu oTTige kaTTalu yAvudE paruSa mADalAguvudilla.

ellara kannaDa 35x35x21 ≈ 25725 nimda 21x21x13 ≈ 5733 gurutugaLavarege 77% gomdalatana kaDitavannu sAdhisidarU, idu innU kaliyalu gamanaharisabEkAda samkhyeya gurutugaLAgide. oTTakSharagaLannu tegeduhAkuvudarimda 21x13 ≈ 273 gurutugaLige mattaShTu kaDitavannu sAdhisabahudu.

ulimAle gumpu (bEre gurutugaLillade) baLakeyalli I sulabhagoLisuvikeyannu tAnE naDeyuva rItiyalli paDeyalAguttade. kelavu samdarbhagaLalli sariyAgiruvikeyalli kaDitavide. Eke, HK yamte kEvala saNNa badalAvaNegaLomdige I dAriyannu anusarisuttade.

Eke ellA ellara kannaDa (ek) ge 31 gurutugaLannu, himdina kAlada gurutugaLannu oLagomDamte kaTTaleya kannaDakke 41 gurutugaLannu mattu imgliSh f, w, z annu jODisalu 44 gurutugaLannu baLasuttade.

Eke yannu ellara kannaDa (ek) sulabhagoLisuvikegaLomdige baLasidAga, "Eke(ek)" padavannu baLasalAguttade.

yAvudE paThyadimda yAvudE badalAvaNegaLillade nEra baraha badalAvaNeyAgiddare, "Eke" padavannu baLasalAguttade.

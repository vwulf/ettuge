# Ellara Kannada Words for Science Terms (Baseline)

## Summary Table

| English Term | Kannada Coinage | Transliteration | Literal Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| Biology | ಜೀವವಿಜ್ಞಾನ | Jivavijnana | Knowledge of life |
| Telescope | ದೂರದರ್ಶಕ | Duradarshaka | Far-shower / makes the distant visible |
| Thermometer | ಉಷ್ಣಮಾಪಕ | Ushnamapaka | Heat measurer |
| Astronaut | ನಭೋನಾವಿಕ | Nabhоnavika | Sky-navigator |
| Photograph | ಬೆಳಕುಚಿತ್ರ | Belakucitra | Light-picture |

**Notes**: These coinages predominantly use Sanskrit-naturalized roots (ಜೀವ, ದರ್ಶಕ, ಉಷ್ಣ, ನಭ, ನಾವಿಕ, ಚಿತ್ರ). The one native Kannada root used is ಬೆಳಕು (light) in Belakucitra.
